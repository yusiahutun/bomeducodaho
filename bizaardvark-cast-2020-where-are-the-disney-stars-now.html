Bizaardvark Cast: Where Are The Disney Stars Now?

September 2024 · 2 minute read

This may be hard to believe, but it’s been more than four years since Bizaardvark first premiered. Yep, on June 24, 2016, fans were introduced to Paige, Frankie and the gang for the first time, and fans cannot believe how fast time has flown by! For those who missed it, the show went on to air for three seasons and 63 episodes, before the iconic Disney Channel show came to its bittersweet end on April 13, 2019.

The fan-favorite series followed two best friends named Frankie and Paige who posted funny songs and videos online under the name Bizaardvark. After reaching 10,000 subscribers on the channel, the girls move into a house with other “Vuuuglers” who frequently post their own viral uploads. The show starred DeVore Ledridge, Ethan Wacker, Olivia Rodrigo, Madison Hu and Jake Paul, and there’s not a day that goes by that fans don’t miss the hilarious antics the kids got into.

“Today is our last day shooting Bizaardvark and I honestly cannot believe it,” DeVore wrote in an emotional Instagram post in September 2018. “We have literally grown up together and its crazy to see how much we’ve all changed and experienced together! Ethan, Olivia and Madison, I love you guys so much. Thank you to every single soul that worked to make this show so enjoyable, not only for kids to watch but for us as actors to work on! This show will forever be my home.”

Over the years, the show became a cult-classic and is available for streaming on Disney+. Since it ended, the stars have all gone to to accomplished some pretty major things. By now, fans know that Olivia not only became a total star in High School Musical: The Musical: The Series, but she also launched a major solo music career.

I have just always been a big songwriter.”
