Harry Styles just went topless on the 2023 Grammys red carpet

It's giving Cirque du Soleil chic

    Harry Styles continues to fly the flag for Gucci in his latest avant garde fashion choice.

    The former One Direction member, rocked up to the 65th Grammy Awards in a sparkling retro-inspired rainbow jumpsuit with a diamond-harlequin pattern. The jumpsuit was paired with patent white cuban heeled boots and his trademark undone hair.

    Harry showed off his tattoos in the Gucci ensemble

    Harry wore the jumpsuit, which is traditionally paired with a blouse or shirt underneath, on its own. The outfit showed off his numerous tattoos (Styles has reportedly racked up over 50 tattoos since first getting inked on his 18th birthday in 2012.)

    The biggest night in music is hosting some of the world's most successful artists at the Crypto.com Arena in Los Angeles for the 65th Grammys. This year's ceremony is being hosted by comedian Trevor Noah for the third time in a row. Harry is performing at tonight’s ceremony which sees him nominated for six Grammys.

    The singer is nominated for six Grammys this year 

    This is the singer's first red carpet appearance of 2023, after a year of stellar looks while promoting his highly-acclaimed turn in Olivia Wilde’s. In 2022 Harry and his then girlfriend Olivia Wilde were the style couple-du-jour, and it wasn’t difficult to work out why. The internationally renowned musician and film director made for an utterly stylish pair, as evidenced by their numerous coordinating Gucci outfits. On the international film festival circuit, the 28-year-old proved himself to be the ultimate muse for the legendary Italian fashion house with a plethora of ultra-sleek, mostly vintage-inspired, tailored looks.

    With the announcement of  Sabato De Sarno taking the reigns as creative director of Gucci, it will be interesting to see if Harry’s  love affair with the brand will continue. De Sarno has some big shoes to fill, his predecessor Alessandro Michele was regarded as the one of the most creative visionaries the house has ever seen and responsible for many of Harry’s most memorable red carpet looks.
