7 New Telugu Movies on Amazon Prime 2024, from Varun Tej starrer Operation Valentine, Om Bheem Bush

April 2024 · 6 minute read

Lately, the latest Telugu movies are hitting the OTT platforms, offering a wide range of choices for the audience. As the current batch of new Telugu movies arrives on Amazon Prime, people can tune in and stream their favorite films right away.

With numerous recent Telugu films already available on Amazon Prime, audiences can go ahead and enjoy an entertaining weekend with their family and friends. So, here are some of the best new Telugu movies that are currently available to stream on Amazon Prime Video.

New Telugu Movies on Amazon Prime 2024

1. Operation Valentine


  • Cast: Varun Tej, Manushi Chhillar, Navdeep, Paresh Pahuja, Ruhani Sharma, Mir Sarwar
  • IMDb Rating: 5.2/10
  • Timeline: 2 hours and 10 minutes

If an evening of Varun Tej Konidela appearing as an Indian Air Force officer is what pleases you, then surely stream the film, Operation Valentine. The latest Telugu movie is an action-thriller based on the 2019 Pulwama Terrorist Attack and the subsequent Balakot Air Strikes in retaliation.

Directed by debutante Shakti Pratap Singh Hada, the film was shot simultaneously in both Telugu and Hindi, marking Varun's debut in Hindi films as well. Moreover, the movie offers an engaging storyline and a wide range of aerial action sequences, which would be loved by fans of the action genre. The film also features actors Manushi Chhillar, Navdeep, Paresh Pahuja, Ruhani Sharma, and many more in key roles.

2. Yatra 2


  • Cast: Mammootty, Jiiva, Ketaki Narayan, Mahesh Manjrekar, Suzanne Bernert
  • IMDb Rating: 6.1/10
  • Timeline: 2 hours and 10 minutes

For fans of biographical movies and political dramas, the latest Telugu movie Yatra 2 is a must-watch. The film depicts the real-life story of the incumbent Andhra Pradesh Chief Minister YS Jagan Mohan Reddy, based on the padayatra (foot march) he undertook from November 6, 2017, to January 9, 2019.

Serving as a direct sequel to the Mammootty-starrer Yatra, which was released in 2019 and based on the life of former Andhra Chief Minister YS Rajasekhar Reddy (Jagan's father), this inspiring film brings back Mammootty reprising his role from the first installment. The movie also boasts an ensemble cast, including Jiiva, Ketaki Narayan, Mahesh Manjrekar, Suzanne Bernert, Subhalekha Sudhakar, Sachin Khedekar, and many more in pivotal roles.

3. Kismat


  • Cast: Abhinav Gomatam, Avasarala Srinivas, Naresh Agastya, Viswadev, Riya Suman
  • IMDb Rating: 6.3/10
  • Timeline: 2 hours and 8 minutes

If you're in the mood for a fun and comedic film, the new Indian movie Kismat is a great option to stream right now. The film follows the story of three ordinary and jobless engineers from a small village. While their lives proceed as usual, things take an unexpected turn when they accidentally become involved in a money heist.

Written and directed by Srinath Badineni, the movie features actors like Abhinav Gomatam, Avasarala Srinivas, Naresh Agastya, Viswadev, Riya Suman, and many more in key roles.

4. Ooru Peru Bhairavakona


  • Cast: Sundeep Kishan, Kavya Thapar, Varsha Bollamma, Vennela Kishore, Harsha Chemudu
  • IMDb Rating: 5.8/10
  • Timeline: 2 hours and 16 minutes

Among the many new Telugu movies on Amazon Prime, here's a supernatural thriller that would satisfy many viewers. The film revolves around the story of a stuntman called Basavalingam, who finds himself in the village of Bhairavakona. The village is infamous for the kidnapping of brides in the region.

During his time there, Basavalingam becomes acquainted with a girl named Geetha. The film further explores how the protagonist unravels the mysteries of the land and the consequences that follow. Led by Sundeep Kishan, the movie also features actors like Kavya Thapar, Varsha Bollamma, Vennela Kishore, Harsha Chemudu, and many more in pivotal roles.

5. Om Bheem Bush


  • Cast: Sree Vishnu, Priyadarshi, Rahul Ramakrishna, Preity Mukundhan, Ayesha Khan, Srikanth, Iyengar, Aditya Menon, Racha Ravi
  • IMDb Rating: 6.3/10
  • Timeline: 2 hours and 30 minutes

Om Bheem Bush is a recently released comedy film. The movie follows the story of three aspiring scientists. Striving to fulfill their dreams, they are mentored by Professor Ranjit. These scientists then embark on a journey to a village where they believe treasure is hidden, but the village already has a black magic practitioner residing there, complicating matters.

Co-written and directed by Sree Harsha Konuganti, the film features actors like Sree Vishnu, Priyadarshi, Rahul Ramakrishna, Preity Mukundhan, Ayesha Khan, and many more in key roles. If you're in the mood for a wacky and fun comedy, then this new Telugu movie on Amazon Prime is a must-watch.

6. Eagle


  • Cast: Ravi Teja, Anupama Parameshwaran, Kavya Thapar, Navdeep, Vinay Rai, Srinivas Avasarala, Madhoo
  • IMDb Rating: 6.4/10
  • Timeline: 2 hours and 39 minutes

If a new Telugu movie filled with action and adventure is your pick for the day - then definitely stream and watch Ravi Teja’s latest Telugu flick Eagle. The film follows the story of Sahadev Varma, an infamous sniper assassin known by the code name "Eagle."

Disguising himself as a common cotton farmer in Talakona, the assassin sets out to bust an illegal arms trade. The situation takes a serious turn when a journalist becomes unknowingly caught up in the web. 

The film starring Ravi Teja in the titular role is written and directed by Karthik Gattamneni with actors Anupama Parameshwaran, Kavya Thapar, Navdeep, Vinay Rai, and many more playing prominent parts in the film.

7. Saindhav


  • Cast: Venkatesh Daggubati, Nawazuddin Siddiqui, Arya, Shraddha Srinath, Ruhani Sharma, Andrea Jeremiah
  • IMDb Rating: 5/10
  • Timeline: 2 hours and 18 minutes

Concluding the list of new Telugu movies on Amazon Prime, we have the Venkatesh Daggubati-starrer action-thriller Saindhav. The gripping movie focuses on the story of a single father with a dark past, who now lives a simple life with his daughter. However, when his daughter requires medical care, he is forced to return to his criminal roots to earn the necessary money.

His return to the criminal underworld and his clash with a ruthless gangster set the stage for the rest of the film's plot. Directed by the acclaimed HIT series director Sailesh Kolanu, the movie also features an ensemble cast, including Nawazuddin Siddiqui, Arya, Shraddha Srinath, Ruhani Sharma, Andrea Jeremiah,, and many more in pivotal roles.

All these new Telugu releases are available for streaming on Amazon Prime Video right now. Moreover, numerous other Telugu releases have also been released on other OTT platforms, ensuring ample entertainment options for you this weekend.
