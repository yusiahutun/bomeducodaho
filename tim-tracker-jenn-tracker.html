Introduction

Tim Tracker is an American YouTuber who is best known for his vlogs reviewing various services he has tried and documenting his everyday life on the YouTube channel – ”TheTimTracker.”

The channel often features the husband-wife duo having fun at theme parks in Orlando, Florida.

Jenn Tracker is an American YouTuber and Tim’s wife, who usually features on the channel TheTimTracker. 

Both are residents of Orlando, Florida.

Biography

He was born on March 1, 1982, in Florida, USA.

Jenn’s birthday was not released to the public.

Tim and Jenn were friends before they started dating.

After returning from college, in 2004, Jenn needed somewhere to stay, and Tim offered to allow her to move in with him.

The two met in high school but were both in relationships at the time.

They hadn’t started their relationship until they’d both graduated college and were both single. The couple said in a video posted on April 2010 that one Halloween, they just decided to give it a try and started dating.

Six years after they started their relationship, Tim was ready to propose to Jenn.

In a November 2010 video, Jenn said:

“He wanted to propose to me at sunset on the beach and I totally ruined it.”

Jenn went on to say:

”I’m like, ‘I don’t want to drive three hours just to go to a dumb beach and see a stupid sunset.” Noting that she wanted to get dinner nearby instead, she said, ‘That’s what I got.'”

On June 3, 2011, the pair got married at Universal Studios in Orlando.

They were married by a justice of the peace on a bridge in front of the Hogwarts building.

When their ceremony was over, the couple rode Harry Potter-themed rides.

One week later, the couple traveled to Ireland to have their marriage blessed at a 13th-century church in Ireland.

YouTube


TheTimTracker channel was created on June 27, 2009.

When Tim and Jenn Tracker started their YouTube channel, it was intended to be a 30-day trial of vlogging their daily lives. However, after the trial period was over, the couple decided to continue the vlogging journey.

As Disney fans, the couple decided to vlog their trips to various theme parks.

Their videos focus mainly on showing off the different attractions and experiences available in the Orlando area.

Tim and Jenn highlight many popular vacation destinations within Orlando, such as Walt Disney World, Universal Orlando, Sea World, and more.

They also travel around the world to vlog at different theme parks and themed cruises.

The couple has traveled to California, Ireland, and even Tokyo.

For their channel’s 10-year anniversary, Jenn said:

“The vlog has been with us for so many different parts and stages of our lives. It’s just incredible.”

Jenn later added:

“We’ve taken tons of awesome trips that never would have happened if it weren’t for YouTube.”
