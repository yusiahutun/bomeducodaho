What to Look For in a Screen Protector in 2023

No matter how careful you are, the screen is the most vulnerable part of your iPhone 14. Even if you never drop your phone, simply using it often enough is all it takes to degrade the quality of your screen.

Any screen protector you choose takes the damage your screen would have otherwise. Tempered glass is the strongest, but it's not the only option. Film screen protectors (often PET plastic) are cheaper and easier to apply, and some people prefer the feel.

In addition to protecting your screen from physical damage, a screen protector protects the oleophobic coating on your iPhone. This helps keep your screen fingerprint-free, but can wear down over time from use. A screen protector preserves this, and tempered glass screen protectors typically have an oleophobic coating of their own.

A screen protector can also be useful from a privacy standpoint. While most screen protectors are just that, some use a polarized feature to keep nearby strangers from seeing what's on your screen. If you frequently deal with sensitive data on your phone, this is an essential feature.

Your screen isn't the only part of your phone made from glass. Your camera lenses are equally important and equally vulnerable. Fortunately, tempered glass screen protectors for the iPhone 14 camera lens are also available, and they're occasionally included with screen protectors.

Of course, to protect your iPhone 14 as much as possible, you should also use a case. While cases may not be as effective as we'd like, they still offer more protection than not using one at all.

While a tempered glass is the material of choice for most screen protectors, they're not for everyone. Some people prefer the feel and touch sensitivity of a film screen protector like the Supershieldz PET 14 Screen Protector.

Supershieldz uses high-quality Japanese PET film, which consists of five layers of protection. This material is easier to install than tempered glass, but earlier film screens would leave residue on your phone's screen. PET screen protectors have come a long way, and Supershieldz says these are residue-free.

The biggest advantage of film screen protectors is the feel and touch sensitivity. To help the overall feel, the top layer of this film screen protector uses HD Clear Coating. This helps your screen stay looking shiny and new, but also feels more seamless to use.

Supershieldz doesn't include an alignment tool for installing these screen protectors. As mentioned above, toolless installation is easier with film screens, but this is something you should note if you're buying this brand of film protectors.

One of the major downsides of film screen protectors is that they're more easily damaged and must be replaced more often. The good news is that Supershieldz includes six film screen protectors in this pack, so you'll have replacements ready for some time.
