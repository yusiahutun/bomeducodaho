Best Yellow Flowers, Plants &amp; Houseplants for Spring and Summer

June 2024 · 3 minute read

Glorious spring days are on the horizon. From lighter mornings to golden-yellow blooms bursting into colour, it's a season of hope, new beginnings and brighter days ahead.

To help you add a little colour to your outdoor space, resident gardening expert and Horticultural Director at Dobbies Garden Centres, Marcus Eyles, has uncovered the best yellow flowers to plant in spring and summer. Inspired by Pantone's Illuminating Colour of the Year 2021, these blooming lovely ideas will help you brighten up your garden in no time.

SPRING

Jacky Parker Photography//Getty Images

Hardy plants are best for the spring season as they can withstand low temperatures, especially those in England. As well as this, spring is the perfect time to plant bedding plants and to get your hands on some gorgeous houseplants, too. Take a look at Marcus' suggestions below...

Hardy plants

1. Forsythia 'Lynwood'

2. Acer 'Orange Dream'

3. Escallonia 'Gold Ellen'

4. Cytisus 'All Gold'

5. Diosma 'Sunset Gold'

6. Euonymous 'Emerald & Gold'

7. Halimium libanotis

8. Philadelphus 'Aureus'

9. Sambucus 'Sutherland Gold'

Bedding plants

10. Primrose Veristar Lemon/Yellow & Primula Belarina Butter Yellow (both new for 2021)

11. Primrose Yellow Orange Eye

12. Pansy Golden Yellow

13. Viola Yellow with Blotch

14. Kinder Young Plant Collection - Yellow Delight
