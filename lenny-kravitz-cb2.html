Lenny Kravitz Debuts Furniture and Home Decor for CB2

June 2024 · 2 minute read

When whispers about Lenny Kravitz‘s first furniture collection—a collaboration with Chicago-based housewares brand CB2—began, we had our doubts. But it turns out, it’s better than we expected.

The 20-piece collection—which hits the brand’s stores and site today—includes lighting ($349), rugs ($199–$1,099), furniture ($229–$999), and home-decor accessories ($34.95–$179) that are inspired by Kravitz’s travel and experiences. Tinged with ’70s undertones and a rocker edge, the range incorporates textiles, leather, wood, metal, and custom patterns that would work equally well in a New York City studio and a California beach house.

Highlights from the collection include a leather-and-brass dining chair ($349) that looks like it was plucked from a Paris flea market and a group of graphic and textural throw pillows ($79.95).
