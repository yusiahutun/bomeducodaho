'Survivor: Island of the Idols' Season 39: Fans say Jamal needs to chill after his abysmal behavior

June 2024 · 3 minute read

'Survivor: Island of the Idols' season 39 returned on October 9 night for a brand new episode and after last week's blindsided elimination from the Vokai tribe left Jamal and Jack really angry. 

Though he had all right to be upset about not being informed about Molly's blindsided elimination, fans reveal his abysmal behavior was uncalled for, especially after he didn't take any of his belongings to tribal council because he was so confident he wouldn't get eliminated. 

Back at the tribe, a very angry Jamal is not ready to forget what the tribe did to him. Even though Tommy was really interested in building the relationship and getting past the tribal council, Jamal reveals that he will forgive but never forget what the tribe did to him, because currently, he feels like he is just a wildcard in the tribe. 

After what happened, Jamal decided that he would go rouge and do whatever keeps him save, and he has plans of getting rid of Dan when the tribe goes to their next tribal council. 

Post the episode, fans took to social media, to reveal their disagreement with Jamal's behavior and warning that if he doesn't let the blindsided elimination pass, he might be the next contestant going home. Fans also went on to add that he had no reason to be annoyed with the elimination because he didn't get anything with him to the tribal council and just slept the entire day.  

Taking to Twitter, a fan wrote, "Oh Jamal... whyyyyy??? He wants agency in the game to call shots and he JUST WAS BLINDSIDED. Now is the time to re-integrate with the group. Take your time and make relationships. He doesn’t need to do this and he’s shooting himself in the foot. #Survivor."

Adding to that another fan said, "Jamal didn't take any of his things to tribal last week. I have no problem with him being left out of the loop. He's a pompous ass with manboobs. #Survivor"

"Damnnn Jamal disappointed me this episode. I support Lauren and Kelly only. The rest of these guys are just yeah #survivor," wrote a fan. While another added, "jamal’s a great player but he’s starting to be a little too sneaky and that might get him in trouble, oh no baby what is you doing #Survivor #Survivor39." 

"#survivor #islandoftheidols Jamal salty over a blindside when he spent the whole day napping - c'mon man," said a fan. Adding to that another wrote. "Also, Jamal doesn’t get to be upset about being left out of last week’s vote. He was taking a nap right after they lost the challenge! #Survivor."
