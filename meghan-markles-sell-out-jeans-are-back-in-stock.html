Meghan Markle's sell-out jeans are back in stock

June 2024 · 2 minute read

As ever with anything Meghan Markle wears, the Mother Denim jeans she wore for her first public appearance with Prince Harry sparked huge interest.

Unfortunately, the brand wasn't aware that Markle was about to step out in that particular style, which was from a past collection and no longer available buy. Although the actress' patronage prompted wider sales across the brand, Mother Denim received a huge number of requests asking if it could reissue the style - a mid-rise skinny with distressed detailing and frayed ankle hems, priced at £160.

So now, six months after Markle wore the Looker jeans to the Invictus Games, the label has reissued them.

“We brought back The Looker Ankle Fray in [the wash] Love Gun that Meghan wore, due to the high demand which came directly from her wearing it,” Lela Becker, the label’s founder, told The Telegraph. “The day after Meghan wore our jeans, we saw nearly a 200% increase in traffic on our website - more people came to motherdenim.com that day than they did on Black Friday.”

Getty Images

Getty Images

Mother Denim has previously been worn by Gigi Hadid, Kendall Jenner and Reese Witherspoon, but Becker says Markle has influenced sales far beyond its other high-profile fans.

“Very occasionally we will recut if there is high demand, this style was popular originally but we were not considering re-cutting it. That was until Meghan wore it and so many people were specially asking for the exact style," said Becker. "We made the decision to re-issue it in December.”
