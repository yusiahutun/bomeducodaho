Hilarious memes show the toll the last 12 months have taken on us

May 2024 · 4 minute read

It's fair to say 2016 has been a mixed year.

It has been tinged with tragedy, with the deaths of idols including Prince, David Bowie, Leonard Cohen, Alan Rickman and Muhammed Ali causing misery for their legions of fans.

And on the political front, this year has seen one of the most divisive US Presidential elections in living memory.

The Brexit vote in Britain sparked a similar wave of feeling, and so it is not surprising that many feel battered and bruised as 2016 nears a close.

This is being marked on Twitter with a series of tweets jokingly illustrating the toll the year has taken on posters.

The memes purportedly show people at the start and end of 2016 - and the change in them is certainly unflattering.
