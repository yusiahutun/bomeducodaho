TikTok's Viral Wavytalk's Curling Brush Promises a Sleek Blowout Look

By now you probably know how easy it is to get sucked down the TikTok rabbit hole. One minute you’re casually scrolling, listening to stories prompted by Susi’s pesto, and the next, you’re six parts deep in a stranger’s scandalous story, feeling fully invested. We’ve admittedly learned a lot on the app and have rushed to buy products after viewing a few too many GRWM tutorials. Our latest obsession? Wavytalk’s 5-in-1 Curling Iron Set , which is being labeled a must-have by multiple influencers.

The hair tool comes with four different wands that snap on and off, and each will produce different sized ringlets or waves. However, the main draw here is actually the curling brush, which has thermal bristles that glide through the hair like a heated round brush, giving users a sleek, smooth, blowout-like look. We’ve seen demonstrations on a variety of hair types, from super curly to pin straight, and each time we reach the end of the clip, we think to ourselves, “It can’t really be that easy to use…can it?”

It turns out the answer is yes. Aside from TikTokers raving about the curling brush, Amazon reviewers are also big fans.

“The thermal round brush works very well and makes achieving a blowout look so simple/quick,” writes one happy customer. “I have a blow dryer round brush as well but hate how loud it is, how long it takes, and how I am sweating by the end of it. This is a nice alternative and also easy to pack when traveling abroad!”

Another positive is that with an under-$50 pricetag, it’s much more affordable than something from Dyson or other popular stylers. The only downside is that it doesn’t dry your hair — just curls and smooths it out — so you can’t use it on wet strands. Still, it’s a great way to freshen up or quickly give your hair some oomph before you head out the door, and with all those interchangeable heads, this 5-in-1 tool might even help you save some space. We’re sold!
