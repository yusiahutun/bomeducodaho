Influencer launches sell-out accessories including viral 'magic' bag and cap

April 2024 · 2 minute read

A fitness influencer was proud to show off her first ever accessories collection that fans couldn't get enough of it.

Gym babe Grace Beverley, who started her career on YouTube, launched a sustainable activewear brand TALA in 2019 to disrupt the fast fashion and activewear industries.

And today, the woman-led fashion brand dropped three major accessories — all below £30.

The collection includes a Classic Tote Bag that goes twice the size of any high street version and features a structured shape with an internal zip pocket for all valuables.

Made responsibly from organic cotton, the £29 bag is designed to "fit your entire life" in.

A travel-friendly pouch is hailed the "viral magic bag" — to many people's surprise, the bag is like the Tardis thanks to the cute moon-shape design.

It's small enough to carry around but it's big enough to fit in a headphone, sunglasses, a digital camera, wallet, a charging back and a deordorant stick.

Grace shared on Instagram and explained how it took her three years to design the perfect cap.

She said: "I'm very picky with caps, I need this to have the perfect amount of curve so I don't look like a backstreet boy.

"I also need this to be sitting at the perfect place on my head so I don't look like a farmer.

"Often it's mixed between a backstreet boy and a farmer and I don't know how so many places get this so wrong.

"Every single cap shape that people would send for us to sample was completely wrong.

"Three f***ing years later, here we are. We've finally designed the perfect cap - curve, shape sits exactly right."

Fans commented on her post and one said: "A TALA cap is what I've been waiting for!"

"If it is the gym/tote bag of dreams I NEED IT NOW!" another added.

Matalan has a similar "moon bag" available on the website for £11 or if you prefer premium material, Marks and Spencer is selling a leather double zip crossbody bag.
