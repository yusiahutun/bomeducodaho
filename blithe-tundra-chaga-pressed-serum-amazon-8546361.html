Shoppers Keep Repurchasing This $20 Firming Serum at Amazon

July 2024 · 2 minute read

I’ve seen a handful of so-called “instant-facelift” products over the years. While many seem to provide an immediate lifting, tightening effect, the results are typically temporary…to the tune of a few hours. A longer-term solution for sagging skin lies in skincare spiked with collagen-proliferating ingredients. 

Collagen — skin’s structural protein — dwindles with age, which contributes to sagging, laxity, and general lack of youthful plumpness. Spurring collagen production, therefore, paves a path to plumper, firmer skin by restoring those structural proteins. Luckily, many anti-aging formulas do just that; among them, this $20 option from Blithe, a Korean skincare brand popularized by its so-called pressed serums. 

The Blithe Pressed Serum Tundra Chaga Instant Face Lift Cream is a dense, nutrient-rich skin solution for lax, aging skin. Despite its name, the “serum” is a two-in-one product that functions as both a glow-inducing moisturizer and potent firming serum. Suitable for all skin types, including sensitive and dry skin, the formula lifts and tightens over time and instantly facilitates a fresh, dewy glow post-application.

As its name suggests, the formula stars chaga mushroom — a fungi variant that can protect skin from environmental damage thanks to its antioxidant properties. ICYMI: Antioxidants, which shield skin from environmental skin-stressors, like pollution, can slow signs of premature aging — like hyperpigmentation, fine lines, wrinkles, and, of course, sagging.

Aside from its hero ingredient, Blithe’s formula contains additional anti-agers with strong scientific support. Notable among them: Beta glucan and adenosine, each of which champion supple, smooth skin. Beta glucan, for one, is an excellent hydrator thanks to its humectant properties, which draw moisture from the surrounding air and into skin. In turn, skin appears instantly plumper. Adenosine, a molecule found naturally in the body, spurs collagen production to tighten, thicken, and firm aging skin.

Per reviewers, this anti-aging trio creates youthful firmness. One shopper calls Blithe’s formula a “facelift in a jar,” noting how it tightened their skin after only two days. A 63-year-old shopper says “the years have dropped off [their] face” since slathering on the pressed serum — which they’ve purchased six times.

For a collagen-supporting solution for sagging skin shop the two-in-one Blithe Pressed Serum Tundra Chaga Instant Face Lift Cream for $20 at Amazon.
