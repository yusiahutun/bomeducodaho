Who is Kirsten Storms?

Kirsten Storms is an American actress. People mostly know her as Zenon Kar in the ‘Zenon’ trilogy. Additionally, she also appeared as Emily in ‘Johnny Tsunami’, and Bonnie Rockwaller in ‘Kim Possible.’

Kirsten Storms’ Early Life, Childhood, and Education

Storms was born in Orlando, Florida as Kirsten Renee Storms on April 8, 1984. She was born to parents Karen Storms and Mike Storms. In addition, she became interested in the world of acting in her childhood years and wanted to be a soap opera actress as a child. She is of American nationality. Furthermore, there are no details available about her ethnic background at present.
