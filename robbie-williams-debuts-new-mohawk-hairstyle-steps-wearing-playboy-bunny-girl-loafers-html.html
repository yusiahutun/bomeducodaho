Robbie Williams debuts a new mohawk hairstyle as he steps out wearing Playboy 'bunny girl' loafers

July 2024 · 3 minute read

He's known for his former 90s hell-raising days.

And Robbie Williams has paid homage to his rocker roots with a new edgy mohawk hairstyle as he attended the AFL Grand Final entertainment line-up at Melbourne Cricket Ground in Australia on Thursday.

The singer, 48, who is set to perform at the show this weekend, also stepped out wearing a pair of £250 Playboy 'bunny girl' loafers by Duke and Dexter.

Robbie opted for full rock star style in a glittering Joker-inspired sequin jacket while wearing one of his signature white vests. 

He showed off his dozens of tattoos while showering himself in several glitzy pieces of jewellery.
