MSNBC and CNN legal analyst Midwin Charles posted that she wasn't 'feeling well' in the weeks leading up to her shocking death at 47.

Charles had taken to Instagram on March 19 to share that she would not be having routinely scheduled Happy Hour. The prominent attorney's family announced that she died Tuesday but did not list the attorney's cause of death. 

'Hey guys! Sadly, happy hour tonight is canceled. I'm not feeling well,' she said in the post. 'See you next Friday.'

'It is with a profoundly heavy heart and the deepest sadness that we announce the untimely passing of our beloved Midwin Charles,' they said in the Tuesday post.
