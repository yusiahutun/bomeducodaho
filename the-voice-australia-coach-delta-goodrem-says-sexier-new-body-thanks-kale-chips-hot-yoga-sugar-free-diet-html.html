They say life begins at 30.

And that seems to be the case for Delta Goodrem, who has says that since reaching the milestone she's never been happier.

The Australian singer opened up to the latest issue of WHO magazine saying she thanks a strict diet and exercise regime for making her 'feel sexier' with her body.

The beauty went on to add that she has been sticking to a mostly-sugar free diet and daily cardio workouts along with hot yoga classes to keep her figure looking super-toned and fit.

Before finding her effective diet, which was suggested by her close pal and U.S. TV host Renee Bargh, the Born To Try singer said would go up and down with her weight.

Delta, who usually sits between a size eight and 10, told the publication she would gain around five kilograms whenever she was recording a new single or album because she found it hard to stick to her diet and workout plans.

Hard work: The singer takes part in daily cardio workouts and hot yoga to keep her figure looking fit and tones 

Target: The Australia singer revealed she used to gain up to five kilograms whenever she would be in the recording studio but has since learnt to carry almonds and kale chips with her at all times

But the singer has since found a way to keep herself on an even keel, keeping sugar-free snacks such as almonds and kale chips close by to manage those sugar-craving moments.

Delta's boost in body confidence has been evident ever since she returned to the television screen earlier this year for the latest series of The Voice Australia. 

Sitting comfortably in her seat alongside international stars Jessie J, Ricky Martin and the Madden brothers, Delta has showed off her slender frame in a range of tight-fitted and revealing outfits. 

Head-turning: Since returning to TV the beauty has regularly flaunted her new super toned body in a range of daring outfits

During last week's battle rounds she flaunted her cleavage and long toned legs in a royal blue gown which featured a plunging neckline and two daring high splits at the front. 

She also poses on the Who cover in a stomach-baring crop top and pants suit combination.

Delta flew back into Sydney earlier this week ahead of The Voice Australia's live shows which kick off on Sunday.
