Different types of Angels weaves to rock in 2024

Not even a hairstylist is aware of a company’s entire hairstyle collection. If you have noticed, there is always an archive they refer to. So, it would be the same for you since you are a novice in the niche, only familiar with what interests you. A refresher of the numerous Angels weaves and their names would come in handy the next time you shop for one. So, where do you start?

1. Rihanna

If you are looking for the ideal short weaves from Angels, you have Rihanna. Of course, the name tells it all, and you are guaranteed something classy that will elevate your look. It is also a timeless piece you can adorn for official and casual occasions. You can also make it classier and more feminine with ornaments and other desired accessories.

2. Ripple curl

Who wouldn't want the ripple effect? The ripple curl is one of the finest Angels weaves you can go for if you are looking for a robust style change. It will give you the oomph you are looking for from a hairstyle. And the good thing is that it is not black but golden brown, a shade that goes well with most people's skin. The weave is of middle length and ends on your shoulder.

3. Rumba

Amongst Angels latest weaves, people have found rumba something worth it. The classic weave look shouts rumba, and of course, it has all the glory that befits the music genre. Given a chance, many ladies would go for this look as it brings out a subtle traditional and, as Kenyans call it, "kienyeji” vibe to your style. The good thing is that it is easy to match with your accessories.

4. Spiral curl

The spiral curl from Angels is an awesome start if you search for curly weaves. You will love the attention to detail and excellence that comes with the hairstyle. Another remarkable thing is that the hairstyle is perfect for any head shape and looks fabulous with a traditional necklace.

5. Sorento

Sorento is the real deal. The look is simple to hack and will only have you spend part of the day at the salon. What most people love about the weave are the double shades. You can have red at the top of your head and black at the sides. And when you go out for that momentous occasion, wear red lipstick to give it a glow. You will love the compliments you receive everywhere you turn.

6. Soft bouncy

If you love keeping it short and cannot forfeit your love for colour, the soft, bouncy hairstyle is for you. The weave mimics a natural afro with curls, hence has a fabulous appeal that you cannot get from any other design.

7. Bongo long

If you are tired of Angels straight weaves, the bongo long will be something you might fancy. It is a short weave that takes the natural shape of your head and hardly goes past your ears. It is great for ladies with a receding hairline as it covers the front section quite well, which you will hardly notice.

8. Sista tempest

The Sista tempest is for the bold, daring, and someone who loves colour too. The hairstyle mimics a mohawk as it is voluminous at the top of the head and slightly reduced on the edges. The curls give the style its elegance and fancy vibe.

9. Princess

If you want a classic princess look, you have the princess weave from Angels. It is straight and clean, going to your shoulders. The middle part makes the hairstyle look awesome. Don't worry if you have a slim face; it will fit perfectly.

10. Passion

Passion is the way to go for ladies with swag. It is a great style for anyone looking to spruce up their look and try something new. The weave has a side part that greatly affects how one looks.

11. Babe cut weave

The babe is perfect. It has all the glam that you are likely looking for. The red colour breaks the black monotony you spot all over on weaves, giving you a unique appearance that will turn heads.

12. Aida

Consider this style named after you if you are called Aida. It isn't only about the name but how it looks. You cannot ignore it as it comes coupled with goodies. The weave is red and short but covers your ears, and the front covers your forehead, nearly going past your eyelashes.

13. Agnes

Agnes is another great Angels weave. It is simple yet stylish. The best way to give it the vibe it desires is to accessorize with African-themed earrings and a traditional necklace. The colour is also awesome as it combines dark and golden brown.

The Hawaiian weave from Angels is one of the longest and most glamorous. It is short of blemishes and almost looks perfect. Anyone would look good with this.
