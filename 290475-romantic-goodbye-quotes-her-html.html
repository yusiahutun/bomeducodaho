Goodbye quotes for her

You may have to say goodbye to your best girlfriend, sister or mother at some point probably because you have to travel for work or studies. It is also possible that you may be moving towns and as such will not be around as much. No matter how painful it is to go away and leave behind your loved ones, you can numb the feelings by leaving them goodbye quotes and messages. You may have to write a few sad goodbye quotes because it is never easy to leave the people you care so much about. Consider these examples.

  • Take heart mama for this is not forever, it is only for a short time and I will be back. Keep praying for me that loneliness will not overwhelm me in a foreign land. Pray that I find favour even when I am so far away. Goodbye mama. Forever in my heart, we will talk every day.
  • Living you my baby is the hardest thing I have to do. I cannot help it because it will be for the betterment of our future. The good thing is that it is only for 3 years and mama will be back to give you a better life. Be good to your grannies, till we see each other again. I will call whenever I can. I love you my sweet girl.
  • I have never stayed so far away from you my sister. You have always been my best friend. My number one fan and encourager. There have never been dull moments in this life because you are always there to make me see the light even when it's all gloomy. This is a chance for me to face life on my own. It is scary but I know that the lessons from my big sis go with me. Pray for me dear, that I get good friends for the time we shall be apart. Goodbye sister.
  • Goodbyes are hard to say because they leave the heart void and pained. My dear friend, since the time we joined this campus together we have been the closest of friends. It’s sad that a time has come when we have to part and chase our dreams. We have worked so hard for the 5 years we have been here and I know we shall be okay. Keep me in your heart and hopefully we can make time for each other whenever possible. I will always be your sister dear girl.

Goodbye quotes for lover

In case you are looking for easy saying goodbye quotes for the one person that you love, then you can be sure that there is no easy way of saying this. While the goodbye is inevitable, you will always feel bad that you have to part. It does not matter if you are parting painfully or out of a good heart. There is never an easy way to say goodbye. Check out these examples to know what to tell your lover when parting.

  • It is so hard to say goodbye because it is not what I want in the first place. It will be so difficult getting over you but what has to be done must be done. Goodbye. It is sad that you are the one that is getting away.
  • My greatest happiness is that even though fate does not want us together, it allowed us some time to experience and create unforgettable memories. For this, I will always cherish and keep them in my heart. Goodbye.
  • This is going to be the hardest period in my life. Having to stay away from you for a few minutes pains me and drives me crazy. I do not know how I will pull through the 6 months I must be away from you. In the end, I believe love will win. Goodbye.
  • I may not want to say goodbye but I have to be brave. I trust in life’s ability to give me a hello, all in due time. Goodbye.
  • Goodbyes are difficult especially if you can never stop loving the person you are saying it to. As unbelievable as it may seem, sometimes, saying goodbye is what we must for this is the greatest way of saying I love you when it hurts so much. Goodbye.
  • I want to thank you for the memories. Though we have to part and it's sad that it had to end this way, you will always be in my heart and mind. Goodbye.
  • My heart ached for a chance to set my eyes on you just for a last goodbye. But I thank God my brain was strong for if I had my way, I would never have the strength to say goodbye. Goodbye.
  • I consider it a privileged to have met someone like you. It started like a joke and within no time none of us could control it. It is so painful but also very fulfilling that I got a chance to experience life for a while with someone that is almost impossible to say goodbye to. We have to do this though, goodbye.
  • Truth be told, I have said so many goodbyes before but none has been harder than you have. Goodbye.
  • We have to solve this problem. The best way to do it is to say goodbye even if it makes my throat hurt.
  • It is okay that you will stay in my heart but out of my life. Goodbye.
  • Saying goodbye may be simple but I am worried about the flashbacks that will come storming when we are gone. May we both find the strength to get through this pain,. for this we must do. Goodbye.
