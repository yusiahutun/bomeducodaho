Elizabeth Prann's Short Bio:

Elizabeth Prann was born on August 9, 1985, in Aurora, Colorado, and is currently aged 31 years. Prann holds a bachelor's degree in journalism from the University of Florida where she was also a member of the woman’s lacrosse team.

Elizabeth Prann is currently a Washington D.C.-based contributor for Fox News. She joined Fox News in 2010 as an Atlanta-based correspondent. Before she served as a reporter for the Fox News Channel, she was a production assistant for On the Record w/Greta Van Susteren for Fox network.
