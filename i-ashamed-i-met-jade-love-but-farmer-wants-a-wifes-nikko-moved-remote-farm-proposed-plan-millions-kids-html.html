It was an unlikely love story. She worked in an office in the middle of the city, while he worked on a remote farm in the middle of nowhere.

And although Farmer Wants A Wife's Nikko Lord said he was 'initially ashamed' he met Jade Truman on the hit Channel Nine dating show in 2012, their love has blossomed into a true romance.

He convinced his paramour to pack her bags and leave Brisbane for him a year ago and now the couple are preparing to tie the knot. However, it hasn't been all plain sailing.

The handsome, loved up pair told OK! Magazine Australia that the first few months were difficult as Jade moved to the farm in North Queensland, 120 km from Richmond, in the middle of a severe drought.
