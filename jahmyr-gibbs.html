Jahmyr Gibbs is a Georgian personality and an American football running back. He is a member of the Detroit Lions in the National Football League (NFL). He played college football for Georgia Tech and spent one year at Alabama. Later in 2023, he was selected by the Lions in the first round of the NFL draft.

Jahmyr Gibbs was born on 20 March 2002 and was raised in Dalton, Georgia. He went to Dalton High School, where he made a record of 897 yards and ten touchdowns as a sophomore. During his junior year, he made the record for 1,431 yards and 20 touchdowns.

As a senior, he made a record for his high school and scored 2,554 rushing yards and 40 touchdowns and was eventually named first-team all-state, the Offensive Player of the Year in the 2020 Georgia 6-A league, and a first-team All-American by Sports Illustrated. In his whole high school career, he made 4,882 rushing yards and 70 touchdowns.

After completing high school, Jahmyr played for Georgia Tech and was offered by various other universities like Michigan, Texas A&M, and North Carolina. During his senior years, he was again rated as a four-star prospect and was ready to be recruited by Florida, Georgia, and Alabama. Still, he chose Georgia Tech over others.
