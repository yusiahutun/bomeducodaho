Mugithi musician KarehB will lay her son to rest today, Friday, April 12.

How KarehB reacted after viewing son Joe Mwadulo

The young mum was smartly dressed in a dark blue dress to escort her son, Joe Mwadulo, on his final journey, and all was well until she reached the casket accompanied by her kin.

While viewing her son's lifeless body in the casket, the musician broke down, leaving netizens online heartbroken.

The musician and her family then proceeded to the funeral ceremony, where they shared their tributes to the young man.

Kenyan celebrities such as Karangu Muraya, Terence Creative, Shix Kapienga, and Teacher Wanjiku were in attendance.

Why KarehB was gifted jersey

The late Joe's friends from Chavakali High School were also in attendance, and they paid their tributes.

A coach of the young man gifted KarehB a jersey to remember her son in a moving tribute.

"All protocols observed. I'm the head coach of basketball, Chavakali. When I took him to be a player, I recognised his potential for shooting. The basketball fraternity in Western loved him. He was voted in as the best player. All the coaches loved him. He used to accept correction. That was one thing I loved about Joe. The basketball fraternity had a small thing for mama, you can be able to remember Joe as a basketball player. "

Kenyans console KarehB over passing of son

Kenyans across the nation united in support of KarehB following the tragic loss of her son in a bus accident.

The devastating incident unfolded as Mwadulo, a student at Chavakali High School, embarked on a journey with classmates aboard an Easy Coach bus.

Mwadulo was the sole victim of the accident, leaving his family and the community reeling in shock and sorrow.
