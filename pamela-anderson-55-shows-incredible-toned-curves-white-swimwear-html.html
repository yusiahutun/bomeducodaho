Pamela Anderson, 55, shows off her incredible toned curves in white swimwear

April 2024 · 3 minute read

Pamela Anderson showed off her incredible toned curves and radiant make-up free visage in a sizzling photoshoot for her collab with Frankies Bikinis. 

The Baywatch star, 55, revealed her ample cleavage and toned midriff as she modeled two different white swimsuits - a sizzling bikini and a sexy one piece with a cut out over her taut tummy.

In one photo, the Love, Pamela star lounges on a cement seashell with a pool in the background. She is gorgeously fresh-faced and her hair is in soft waves. 

The second suit featured hip-hugging bottoms with fringe hanging from the hips with the star striking a sultry pose.

The snaps were captioned: 'surf bunny. a super soft washed cotton like your favorite vintage tee, lined with swim fabric #pamxfrankies.'
