Apple's silicone case for the iPhone 12 Pro Max comes equipped with MagSafe technology. This means you can charge your iPhone, wirelessly, with a MagSafe-compatible charger without taking the case off.

It's a pricey choice at $49, but it is the best choice if you're looking for an official Apple option to one of the best iPhone 12 Pro Max cases you can buy today.

The Design

If you've ever owned an Apple silicone case, then at first glance, the Apple iPhone 12 Pro Max Silicone Case with MagSafe won't look much different. However, turn it over, and you'll notice a small circle inside the case that makes the built-in magnet ring easily identifiable.

Rather than sticking the magnet onto the case like some brands have done, Apple has been in keeping with its simplistic design and built it into the case for a sleeker finish. And although the magnets aren't particularly strong, they have been designed to align your phone perfectly with a MagSafe charger to ensure your iPhone charges in a snap (literally).

The other notable design change from the Apple iPhone 11 Silicone Case is that the opening at the bottom is filled in, allowing just the speakers and the Lightning port to be exposed. This ensures every inch of your iPhone is protected, although it does mean a little more care and attention is required to get the cable to fit snuggly, and the case does have a habit of shifting slightly if you put pressure on it in certain places.

If you're looking to add a little more color to your iPhone, you'll be pleased to know that the Apple iPhone 12 Pro Max Silicone Case with MagSafe is available in a wealth of colors, from black to plum, cantaloupe to Capri blue.

Apple's Silicone Case in Action

Since silicone is somewhat of a bendy material, the Apple iPhone 12 Pro Max Silicone Case with MagSafe is pretty straightforward to put on. It fits securely around the iPhone 12 Pro Max with little effort, keeping the volume and lock buttons protected, while exposing the Ring/Silent switch for easy access.

The case is soft to touch but provides enough grip so it doesn't feel like your iPhone is slipping out of your hands. Apple knows how to make cases, and they've designed this case to tick all of the boxes, and then some. However, if you tend to put your phone in your pocket, you'll soon notice that dust and fluff get attracted to the silicone material quite quickly.

There's a small lip around the camera cutout to ensure the cameras are protected if you place your phone with the screen facing upwards. It makes it easy to avoid scratches, but if you were to drop it from a height or at an angle, there's probably not enough cushion to prevent all damage, like some of the more rugged iPhone 12 Pro Max cases available.

Comfort and Reliability

Weighing just over 2.5 ounces, the Apple iPhone 12 Pro Max Silicone Case with MagSafe is barely noticeable. It doesn't add much extra bulk to the iPhone which is a relief since the iPhone 12 Pro Max weighs 8 ounces; a hefty option on its own.

The case is easy to install, with very little effort required in comparison to some other iPhone 12 Pro Max cases.

The Best Option

If you're not fussed by fancy extras then the Apple iPhone 12 Pro Max Silicone Case with MagSafe is 100% worth the investment.

It's not going to slip out of your hands, it protects the camera well, and its shock-absorbent design makes it a great choice for everyday use.
