The Evolutionary Line

Metang is the middle stage evolution of Beldum and evolves into Metagross at level This evolutionary line has become popular among trainers due to their impressive stats and versatile move sets.

Weight and Height

Metang weighs approximately 202.5 lbs (91.6 kg) and stands at a height of 3’11” (1.2 m). Its heavy body and sturdy build contribute to its durability in battles.

Steel Typing

Being a Steel-type Pokémon, Metang has a robust defense against a variety of attacks, including Normal, Rock, Ice, Fairy, Psychic, Dragon, Flying, and Bug.

Psychic Powers

In addition to its steel typing, Metang also possesses psychic powers, allowing it to harness telekinetic abilities and manipulate objects with its mind.

Ability: Clear Body

Metang’s ability, Clear Body, prevents its stats from being lowered by the effects of an opponent’s moves or abilities. This gives it an advantage in battles against Pokémon that rely on stat-reducing moves.

Meteor Mash

Metang is known for its signature move, Meteor Mash, which deals heavy damage to opponents and has a chance to increase its own attack stat.

High Defense Stat

One of Metang’s notable attributes is its impressive defense stat, making it resilient against physical attacks.

Weakness to Fire and Ground

Despite its solid defenses, Metang is vulnerable to Fire and Ground-type moves. Trainers should be cautious when facing Pokémon with these types.

Genderless Pokémon

Metang, like its pre-evolution Beldum, is a genderless Pokémon. It cannot breed with other Pokémon except for Ditto.

Evolutionary Stone Requirement

In order to evolve Beldum into Metang, and subsequently into Metagross, a Metal Coat must be used as an evolution item.

Steelworker’s Tactics

In battle, Metang often employs strategic and analytical thinking to outmaneuver its opponents, utilizing its strong defenses and powerful moves to dominate the battlefield.

Magnet Pull

Metang has the ability Magnet Pull, which prevents Steel-type Pokémon from fleeing battles or switching out, making it an effective choice against those with a Steel typing.

Rare Encounter

Metang is considered a rare Pokémon in the wild and is often encountered in special locations or during specific weather conditions.

Found in Sinnoh Region

In the Pokémon games, Metang can be found in various locations across the Sinnoh region, such as Iron Island and Mt. Coronet.

Legendary Connection

Metang’s evolution line, specifically Metagross, has a connection to the Legendary Pokémon, Cosmog. This connection is explored in certain storylines and events in Pokémon games.

Aerial Agility

Despite its heavy body, Metang is surprisingly agile in the air, often performing aerial maneuvers with grace and precision in battle.

Team Rocket’s Metang

In the Pokémon anime series, a Metang was captured and used by Team Rocket. This Metang showcased its immense power and intelligence during battles.

Trading Opportunity

Metang is one of the Pokémon that requires trading to evolve. By trading Metang, trainers can obtain a rare Pokémon and complete their Pokédex.

Breeding Compatibility

Metang is able to produce eggs when bred with a Ditto. This allows trainers to obtain more Metang and potentially breed Pokémon with desired traits.

Champion’s Favorite

Metang is known to be a favored Pokémon among champions in various Pokémon leagues. Its strength, intelligence, and adaptability make it an ideal choice for skilled trainers.
