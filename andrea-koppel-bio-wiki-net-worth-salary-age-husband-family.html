Regarding her education, she attended Stone Ridge School of the Sacred Heart and received a bachelor degree in political science from Middlebury College with a concentration in the Chinese language and Asian studies.

Koppel started her career as the network’s Tokyo reporter in 1993. After two years in, Koppel became CNN’s Beijing Bureau Chief. Then from 1998 to March 2006, she worked as a network’s State Department correspondent. In 2008 she joined M+R Strategic Services as a chief which is a Washington DC-based public relations company.
