Sienna Miller's perfect striped summer suit

October 2024 · 3 minute read

Event dressing in the summer months can be tricky, but not for Sienna Miller. She's been a go-to for celebrity-style inspiration for years, thanks to her ability to always nail a dress code.

The actress was spotted attending Wimbledon this weekend in the perfect summer look; a striped slouchy suit. It's an outfit that's easy to master and, should you invest in one yourself, we guarantee it will fast become one of your favourite wears whenever the summer rolls around.

Below, we break down Miller's Wimbledon look and highlight how to make it work for you.

Karwai Tang//Getty Images

The look

Miller wore a blue and striped linen trouser suit from Ralph Lauren with a flattering tie-up waist, styled with a navy-coloured woven bag, timeless black sunglasses and platform heels. She kept her jewellery simple and daytime-appropriate with a pair of silver Missoma hoop earrings.

Why it works

While at first a trouser suit on a hot summer's day at the tennis championships might sound unappealing, Miller's take on the style is perfect for the warmer weather because the loose-fit linen makes it breathable in higher temperatures. Teaming her two-piece with black platform heels and matching sunglasses gives her outfit an instant cool-girl spin.

Neil Mockford//Getty Images

Celebrity Maeve Rielly has an easy formula for those attempting to tackle more oversized tailored looks. "Keep the shirt underneath the blazer tight or keep something fitted so that you can see the body in some way," she advises. "I do that a lot with baggy trousers, a crop top, and then a baggy shirt or blazer." This prevents your frame from getting completely lost in excess material. Or, be inspired by Miller and go bare underneath the blazer for an effortlessly elegant option.

Why it's a forever classic

It comes as no surprise that Miller's trouser suit is a wardrobe classic, thanks to its timeless striped print and trend-free fit. You'd be able to wear this ensemble for decades to come, no matter what season, which makes it a smart wardrobe investment and without a doubt a forever classic.

Neil Mockford//Getty Images

How to style it

The beauty of the trouser suit is it can be worn just about anywhere: weekends with trainers and a t-shirt, daytime event dressing a la Miller with platform heels and nothing underneath, or simply add your favourite party heels, some statement jewellery and a sleek upd0 for an evening soirée.
