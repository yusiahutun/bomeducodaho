BAZAAR Beauty Icon Awards 2023

April 2024 · 12 minute read

Over the past few years, thousands of new beauty products have landed on store shelves, promising groundbreaking results—but only a handful have earned icon status. The common thread between this elite group? Science-led and technology-focused innovation, which are two of the biggest trends moving the needle right now, says cosmetic chemist Julio Lamberty. Ahead, our Harper's Bazaar Beauty Icon winners across skincare, hair care, makeup, and fragrance.

SKIN

Inside these jars and vials is cutting-edge science for glowing skin.

FRESH DELIVERY

Olay Super Serum

Olay Super Serum is fueled by a proprietary niacinamide, and the formula turns from an iridescent purple to clear once it’s fully absorbed—so you know exactly when to move on to your next skincare step.

FRESH DELIVERY

Noble Panacea The Absolute Nourishing Lift Oil

Noble Panacea gained traction for its patented Organic Super Molecular Vessel, developed by the brand’s founder, organic chemist and Nobel Laureate Sir Fraser Stoddart. The molecule houses and protects skincare actives to preserve their efficacy, and in the brand’s new the Absolute Nourishing Lift Oil, it combines moisturizing oils with time-released hyaluronic acid, delivering unprecedented nourishment.

FRESH DELIVERY

Paula's Choice 25% Vitamin C + Glutathione Clinical Serum

Vitamin C is notorious for being unstable, but Paula's Choice 25% Vitamin C + Glutathione Clinical Serum utilizes an oil-and-water soluble version of the ingredient to ensure the antioxidant stays potent. The formula also boasts glutathione, which doubles down on antioxidants and helps reduce irritation (a common side effect of vitamin C).

FRESH DELIVERY

Bonjout Beauty Le Balm

This solid serum-balm is packed with 68 skincare actives to deliver unprecedented radiance, glow, and bounce. Developed by a French Pharmacist, it's even safe for sensitive skin. Slather it on as the last—or only—step in your nighttime skincare routine.

FRESH DELIVERY

Onélogy HPR RETINOID 0.3%

We've never seen a retinoid like this before: pill-like tablets that you add water to in the palm of your hand to transform into a serum. The genius packaging makes this the most travel-friendly skincare we've ever tried.

PLUMP IT UP

Shiseido Bio-Performance Skin Filler Serums

Shiseido Bio-Performance Skin Filler Serums have a two-pronged approach: a nighttime formula, which delivers shrunken hyaluronic acid molecules deep into the skin, and a morning formula to restore them to their original size, dramatically plumping the complexion.

PLUMP IT UP

Dr Dennis Gross DermInfusions Fill + Repair Serum

Dr. Dennis Gross Skincare DermInfusions Fill + Repair Serum sends hyaluronic acid, peptides, niacinamide, and ectoin deep into skin to soften fine lines.

PLUMP IT UP

StriVectin Anti Wrinkle Peptide Plump Serum

Now 15% Off

A proprietary peptide in StriVectin Anti-Wrinkle Peptide Plump Line Filling Bounce Serum activates the proteins that create collagen, elastin, and hyaluronic acid to soften the appearance of wrinkles.

REINVENTED STAPLES

Pavise Dynamic Age Defense SPF

Pavise Dynamic Age Defense SPF sunscreen targets not just UV rays and blue light but also havoc-wreaking reactive oxygen species, a type of molecule that further damages cells and DNA

REINVENTED STAPLES

Tower 28 Beauty SOS Daily Skin Barrier Redness Recovery Moisturizer

Tower 28 reimagined its cult-favorite SOS calming spray as the moisturizing SOS Daily Barrier Recovery Cream, which hits the reset button on stressed skin with soothing allantoin.

REINVENTED STAPLES

Lisa Eldridge Skin Enhancing Treatment Cleanser

Lisa Eldridge Seamless Skin Skin Enhancing Treatment Cleanser is powered by saponins, plant-derived soaplike cleansing agents that date back to ancient Rome. It also doubles as a hydrating mask. (One use can increase moisture levels by 73 percent.)

TAKE IT HOME

Beyond Miracles Miracletox Perfection Mesocare Tox Ampoule

Microneedling is the gold standard for in-office treatments that address texture and wrinkles. At home, there’s Beyond Miracles Miracletox Perfection Mesocare Tox Ampoule, single-use serum ampoules infused with patented liquid microneedles that create microchannels in the skin to stimulate the production of collagen and elastin.

TAKE IT HOME

RéVive Fermitif Chin Contour Instant Tightening Peptide Mask

To complement the jawline tightening treatments you can find at your derm's office, this face mask helps lift and firm the area thanks to a blend of potent peptides and a snug fit.

SMARTER SERUMS

Orveda The Omnipotent Concentrate

Orveda The Omnipotent Concentrate not only stops cellular senescence from spreading, with biotech-derived antioxidants and bio-fermented yeast, it delays their formation.

SMARTER SERUMS

Dior Dior Prestige Le Nectar Premier

A recent focus of skincare science is senescent cells, which are almost zombie cells that send deleterious aging signals to surrounding healthy ones. Dior Prestige Le Nectar fights senescent cells with a unique rose-and-peptide complex to add a youthful lift and glow to skin.

From repairing damage to soothing your scalp, these must-haves deserve a spot on your vanity.
