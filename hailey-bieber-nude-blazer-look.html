Hailey Bieber Goes Nude in a Butterscotch Blazer for Date Night

August 2024 Â· 2 minute read

Hailey Bieber loves a monochromatic look.

Last night, the model was seen leaving popular L.A. restaurant Craig's wearing yet another blazer ensemble, only this time in a more neutral hue. Captured by the paps with husband Justin Bieber by her side, she sported an oversized leather blazer from cult retailer Frankie Shop with a nude-colored slipdress underneath. To no one's surprise, the May cover star toted the large woven pouch bag from Bottega Veneta in a similar shade.

Hailey dressed the outfit up with a pair of strappy lace-up sandals by celebrity-approved footwear brand Femme and a pair of medium-sized hoops from her go-to brand Jennifer Fisher. Per the model's Instagram Stories, she sported a gloss-only makeup look and skipped her signature loose waves, opting instead for a '90s-inspired half-up-half-down do. Though her hubby kept it casual for their night out, the model obviously still embraced a full look for the occasion.

It's no secret that the former Baldwin has donned more than her fair share of blazers over the years. Gravitating toward those of the oversized variety, with padded shoulders and hourglass shapes, she, along with stylist Maeve Reilly, have proven just how versatile the smartly tailored piece can be. Between wearing the garment with skinny slacks for a business meeting and paired with denim shorts and heels, the street style star has proven time and time again that she can throw on the outwear piece to complete just about any look.
