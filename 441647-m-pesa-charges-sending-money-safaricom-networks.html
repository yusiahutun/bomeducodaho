What M-PESA services are free?

These M-PESA transactions are free:

  • Depositing money to your M-PESA account.
  • M-PESA registration.
  • Buying airtime through M-PESA.
  • M-PESA balance inquiry.
  • Changing your M-PESA PIN.

Note that one can deposit funds only into an M-PESA account registered under their identification document (ID). Depositing directly into third-party accounts is not allowed.

How do you send cash on M-PESA?

You can send money between on Safaricom M-PESA using your phone's M-PESA sim toolkit or the M-PESA mobile app. Alternatively:

  • Dial *334#.
  • Select "send money."
  • Enter the recipient's phone number
  • Enter the amount of money.
  • Enter your M-PESA PIN, then send.

How do you send cash from M-PESA to Airtel Money?

You can send money from M-PESA to Airtel Money using Pesapal.

  • Go to your phone's M-PESA menu.
  • Select "Pay Bill."
  • Enter business no. 220220.
  • Enter account no. AIRTXXXXXX (XXXXXX is your Airtel phone number).
  • Enter the amount.
  • Enter your M-PESA PIN, then send.

How do I transfer money from Safaricom M-PESA to Telkom?

You can send money from M-PESA to Telkom's T-kash customers and Airtel Money subscribers using this process:

  • Dial *334#.
  • Select "Send Money."
  • Select "Send to other Network."
  • Enter your Telkom or Airtel mobile number.
  • Enter the amount.
  • Enter your M-PESA PIN, then send.

How much does M-PESA charge for international money transfers?

You do not pay to use M-Pesa International Money Transfer services, but the prevailing forex rates apply. Visit a Safaricom IMT partner to send cash abroad.

How to know M-PESA charges?

Dial M-PESA USSD shortcode *334# to view charges per transaction or use the M-PESA cost calculator on the "My Safaricom App."

What are the M-PESA Global transaction limits?

You can send a minimum of Ksh. 101 and a maximum of Ksh. 70,000 via the M-PESA International Money Transfer services.

Why can't I send over Ksh. 35,000 to unregistered M-PESA subscribers?

How to reverse a KCB transaction sent to a wrong number

You can withdraw up to Ksh. 300,000 in a day from an M-PESA agent.

Is it possible to reverse an M-PESA transaction?

Safaricom can reverse money sent to the wrong recipient back to your M-PESA account if you report the issue to them on time.

How do I reverse an M-PESA transaction?

Dial 100 and select the option to speak to a Safaricom customer agent or send the M-PESA transaction message to 456 to initiate the M-PESA self-reversal process.

Can you reverse M-PESA after 24 hours?

To reverse an M-PESA transaction after 24 hours, dial 100 and choose to speak to a Safaricom customer agent. The agent will assist you with the reversal.

Is there a time limit for M-PESA reversal?

You can only use the M-PESA self-reversal process within two hours after sending money to the wrong recipient. Send the whole M-PESA transaction message to 456 to initiate the self-reversal process.

How much does M-PESA charge to withdraw from PayPal?

You will be charged a 3% conversion fee for each PayPal withdrawal and top-up transaction. In addition, for top-up transactions, M-PESA will charge you a customer-to-business PayBill charge as detailed in Safaricom's terms and conditions.

How to join M-PESA Global?

Dial *840# and accept the terms and conditions to register with M-PESA Global. Select the "send money abroad" option and follow the prompts to complete the process.

Safaricom's M-PESA charges are deducted from your account's balance - meaning the fee is subtracted from the money remaining in your M-PESA account, not the amount you wish to send. You earn Bonga Points from the M-PESA fee charged when you transact.
