FOPE, Italian fine jewellery

The past year has seen many of us drastically re-examine our values, particularly in regard to the way we consume fashion. The issues with wear-once fast fashion have become all-too clear and, in one of the few upsides to the last 12 months, the focus has shifted towards investing in timeless pieces with longevity.

Those looking to make a new-season investment should direct their attention toward FOPE. Beginning life as an Italian goldsmith in the 1920s, FOPE has spent the last century carving out a reputation as one of Europe's most coveted fine jewellery names, available in more than 50 countries and having recently opened a flagship boutique on London’s Old Bond Street.

Recognisable by its signature Novecento mesh chain design - a design centred around a quintessentially Italian idea of bold, elegant style - FOPE offers a range of necklaces, bracelets, rings, earrings and watches finished in 18kt white, pink and yellow gold, and embellished with delicate smatterings of pavé diamonds.

Open Gallery

FOPE is also known for its groundbreaking Flex’it technology. Bracelets and rings
are crafted with an internal system of intricate solid gold springs, affording pieces the flexibility to bend and move with the wearer. The brand updated the mechanisms, first crafted in its atelier in the 1950s, in 2007, and has made delicate modifications to the technology since, radically transforming gold jewellery into easy to wear and smooth accessories. Think: the elegance of timeless gold jewellery combined with the comfort of a perfect pair of vintage jeans.

Those looking to invest in FOPE’s coveted designs need look no further than the brand’s newly launched Essentials collection, a range of 18kt gold rings and earrings inspired by the Novecento mesh chain and designed to be worn with other FOPE creations from past and future collections. The precious rings, which come in three different widths and gold colour combinations, can be stacked together and the Flex’it technology allows you to wear the ring wherever you choose. Sizing is no longer a problem, which also makes it the perfect gift. Meanwhile, the earrings – available in three sizes of classic hoops, two sizes of studs and a newly launched Flex’it mesh version – can also be mixed and matched according to the wearer’s preferences. As for the bracelet, there's no clasp needed – you simply roll it onto your wrist and go, creating the perfect companion to any day and night outfit.

FOPE

Aside from offering a smart take on traditional gold jewellery, FOPE pieces are also made with a commitment to sustainability. In 2014, the brand became a certified member of the Responsible Jewellery Council, guaranteeing the quality and traceability of its gold and diamonds and the ethical production of its jewellery.

As a heritage brand only a few years shy of its centenary, FOPE’s ongoing success is testament to the enduring appeal of intelligent, versatile design and the everyday luxury of Italian design. As the world looks optimistically toward a future in which international travel is once again a shared reality, many of us are dreaming of summers spent on the Italian coast, absorbing the country’s unparalleled brand of high-octane glamour. Until then, we’ll be getting our dose of la dolce vita courtesy of FOPE’s Essentials collection, and its many new collections to come.

In the UK, FOPE is stocked by the best independent jewellers nationwide and a selection of Mappin & Webb and Goldsmiths stores. The FOPE flagship boutique is located on Old Bond Street. Visit fope.com to discover more
