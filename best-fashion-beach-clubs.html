The best designer fashion beach pop-ups for summer 2023

July 2024 · 5 minute read

We earn a commission for products purchased through some links in this article.

Courtesy of Louis Vuitton x Mandarin Oriental

1

Dior at the One&Only Desaru Coast in Malaysia, and at the Surin Phuket hotel in Thailand

Could there really be anything chicer than a Toile de Jouy sunlounger? This summer, Dior is taking over the coastline at the elegant Ember Beach Club of the One&Only Desaru Coast in Malaysia, and at the Surin Phuket hotel in Thailand. Expect a palette of summery, ice-cream hues (think pink-and-white striped cabanas), life-sized sculptures of wild animals and, of course, 'Dioriviera' pools in which to take a designer dip. The Dior Café at the Ember Beach Club offers a Mediterranean menu that's pure seaside perfection; follow up with a spot of outdoor mahjong and table tennis.

Open until the end of summer 2023. BOOK the One&Only Desaru Coast now and BOOK the Surin Phuket hotel now.

2

Missoni at the One&Only Resort in the Maldives

Transport yourself to a tropical island paradise via One&Only Reethi Rah in the Maldives, which has teamed up with Missoni to bring the iconic Italian brand's instantly recognisable, colourful prints to the dreamy destination. Signature zigzags now adorn the resort's Beach Club, from parasols to loungers, cabanas and towels; you'll also find Missoni's stylings in some of the villas, as well as on the transport buggies, paddleboards and bicycles. There's even a Missoni-adorned popsicle stand (just the thing for an afternoon treat).

Open until the end of summer 2023. BOOK the One&Only Reethi Rah now.

3

Loro Piana at the La Réserve à la Plage in Saint Tropez

For the ultimate 'quiet luxury' break, look no further than Loro Piana's collaboration with Saint Tropez's La Réserve à la Plage beach club, which boasts an unbeatably elegant yet relaxed vibe. Mirroring the house aesthetic, you'll find the brand's iconic colours and patterns – burnt orange, caramel brown, milky cream and lots of stripes – on parasols, beach towels and at the bar-restaurant. Like what you see? You'll be able to shop the brand's Resort 2023 collection at the personalised beach club boutique.

Open until the end of summer 2023. BOOK La Réserve à la Plage now.

4

Fendi at the Puente Romano Beach Resort in Marbella

The world’s first Fendi beach club has thrown open its doors – well, candy-striped awnings – at the five-star Puente Romano Beach Resort in Marbella. The Italian fashion house has taken over the hotel’s popular Chiringuito club for the summer, giving it a luxurious makeover complete with Fendi-patterned loungers, tableware and beach chairs. There's even a private cabana you can book for the day (the ultimate in designer relaxation). Pop by the brand’s adjacent pop-up store, which stocks key pieces from the new Astrology collection alongside house signatures, such as the Peekaboo and Baguette bags.

Open until the end of summer 2023. BOOK the Puente Romano Beach Resort now.

5

Dolce & Gabbana

In a match made in fashion heaven, Dolce & Gabbana has joined forces with the San Domenico Palace in Taormina (yes, the filming location for The White Lotus) to create a beach club of epic opulence. A complete redesign of the pool area sees striped umbrellas, sunloungers patterned in the house's signature blue-and-white print, and matching bar carts loaded with granita for full-on la dolce vita vibes. And with views like these, who wouldn't want to stay all summer?

Open until the end of summer 2023. BOOK the San Domenico Palace now.

6

Jacquemus at the Indie Beach in St Tropez

Staying true to the brand's French roots, Jacquemus has set up a glorious, sunshine-yellow beach club at Indie Beach in St Tropez, open until October. You'll be met by lemon-hued stripes as far as the eye can see, on surfboards, umbrellas and loungers – that is, of course, until you reach the turquoise waters. If you need a moment to cool off from the heat, the Été collection (which translates, naturally, as 'summer' in French) is stocked at the cool and calm Jacquemus store nearby – and there are plenty of raffia bags, printed towels and sleek sunglasses that'll look right at home on the shores.

Open until 10 October 2023. BOOK Indie Beach now.

7

Louis Vuitton at the Mandarin Oriental in Bodrum

Many fashion houses choose the well-trodden shores of France or Italy for their beach clubs, but for something slightly different, consider venturing to Bodrum, where Louis Vuitton is popping up for the summer. The brand is taking over the Mandarin Oriental’s ‘Blue Beach’, staying true to its name with cool, azure tones decorating sunloungers and cushions, with raffia and straw accents adding a dash of seaside charm. By day, you’ll have clear vistas of Bodrum’s bay; by night, you’ll be treated to the most spectacular sunsets. The new hotel boutique, inspired by the Maison’s latest ‘By the Pool’ collection, incorporates fresh tones and neutral materials, so you’ll be outfitted for all occasions in the city.

Open until the end of summer 2023. BOOK the Mandarin Oriental Bodrum now.

8

Valentino at the Palazzo Avino in Italy

This year, Valentino is returning to the shores of its Italian homeland and partnering with the hotel Palazzo Avino, on the Amalfi Coast of Ravello, to create a beach hideaway like no other. Decorated in the Maison’s signature red, the club is perched on the cliffs, so you’ll have the most incredible views of the deep blue sea. There’s also a Valentino boutique on the resort’s grounds, which stocks the brand’s Escape 2023 collection: the kaftans and beachwear are not to be missed.

Open until 31 September 2023. BOOK the Palazzo Avino now.

9

Gucci at the Loulou à Ramatuelle in France

On the picturesque shores of Pampelonne, Loulou à Ramatuelle is the backdrop for Gucci’s summer-bright pop-up. Celebrating the launch of Gucci Summer Stories, an escape-inspired collection, this beach club is a dreamy escape of lemon-striped parasols and sunloungers printed with the fashion house’s famous green-and-red branding. The on-site boutique offers the ideal, immersive shopping experience, where you’ll be able to pick up accessories that will take you from beach to city.

Open until the end of September 2023. BOOK Loulou à Ramatuelle now.
