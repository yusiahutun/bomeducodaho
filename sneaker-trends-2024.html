13 Trending Sneakers for Women to Shop Now

February 2024 · 7 minute read

Trends usually fall into one of two categories. The first? Trends you buy and try but may not have a long-lasting relationship with. The second? Trends so enduring and game-changing they end up transforming your wardrobe. While many seasonal trends feel fleeting, most of the spring 2024 sneaker trends we’ve spotted fall into that coveted second category. Sure, these trending sneaker styles feel fresh and of-the-moment. But they’re also functional, classic, and comfortable—the kind of thing you’ll end up wearing on repeat long after the season is done. 

Finding the right pair of sneakers is no small task. Sneakers are a commitment. You can’t really switch them up after leaving the house, like you can with your hair or jewelry. Once you slip on your sneakers, you’re wearing them all day—making it essential to get sneaker-shopping right. The best sneakers are stylish, supportive, and comfortable. They fit right, play well with your wardrobe, and should last you at least a year of wear and tear. 

Luckily, great sneakers abound—and they’re becoming easier to identify. Brands like New Balance, Adidas, and Reebok have nailed the function-to-fashion ratio with sneakers that will win over both trendsetters (like Taylor Swift) and dads. The trending sneakers they’re releasing are perfect for everyday wear, sure to upgrade your favorite athleisure ensembles and complement your cutest outfits.

This spring, you can expect to see 2024’s favorite color trends and clothing trends reflected in your footwear. Metallic sneakers are having a moment, and Barbiecore is still going strong. The spring 2024 sneaker trends we’re eyeing range from classic white trainers to cozy knit slip-ons, giving everyone a way to get their kicks.

  • Classic White

    White sneakers will be a trend every single year so you can never go wrong with getting a new pair. I personally love styling athletic white sneakers with a suit set or a maxi skirt for days I know I’ll be walking a lot around the city.

  • Metallic Kicks

    Metallics have been trending for a minute now. Last season, metallic heels and ballet flats were two of the most popular shoe choices on the market. In 2024, metallics will be taking over the sneaker world. I love this trend because there are so many different ways to wear it. You can wear sequined sneakers, white sneakers with metallic accents, or all-over silver. This is also a fun shoe choice for a night out in lieu of heels.

  • Green Footprint

Forest green is a big color trend for 2024 and the shade has made its way into the sneakersphere. Since green is a color that is commonly found in nature, it’s very easy to wear with other neutrals and colors—AKA you can wear green sneakers with everything you already own.
