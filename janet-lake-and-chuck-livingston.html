Janet Lake and Chuck Livingston

February 2024 · 2 minute read

Chuck Livingston and Janet Lake were married for 12 years. They dated for 1 year after getting together in Jul 1960 and married on 25th Nov 1961. 12 years later they divorced in Sep 1974.

They had a daughter named Paige age 61.

Janet Lake is a 87 year old American Actress. Born Janet Mary Lenkey on 11th March, 1936 in Norristown, Pennsylvania, USA, she is famous for The Life and Legend of Wyatt Earp. Her zodiac sign is Pisces.
