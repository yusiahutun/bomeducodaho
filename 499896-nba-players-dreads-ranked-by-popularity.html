The NBA is well known for its competitiveness, wealth and talent. In addition, the league is also famous for its sporting personalities and its sense of fashion. In the past, most players kept it simple regarding hairstyle, with many spotting bald heads or extreme afros. However, in recent times, there has been an emergence of stars with dreadlocks. So which ones are they? And which are the most popular NBA players with dreads in 2024?

The NBA has grown in popularity in recent times. It is the second most followed sport in the USA, behind the NFL and after baseball. With such publicity comes attention. Players use this publicity to promote individual or corporate brands. Therefore, it is no surprise that most stars spot new hairstyles like dreadlocks or donning the latest fashion.

NBA players with dreads

If you are a fan of the NBA, you might have noticed that most players nowadays spot a similar and unique hairstyle, the dreadlocks. From the 1989 number-one draft pick, Pervis Ellison, to the 2011 round-one pick, Jimmy Butler, the league has witnessed many dreadlocked players.

But in 2024, which famous NBA players have dreadlocks? Here are 20 names ranked on their popularity and fame.

1. Ja Morant

Jamel "Ja" Morant is an American professional basketball player for the Memphis Grizzlies of the NBA. He is among the best young players In the NBA with dreadlocks. Ja Morant constantly steals the show with his unique hairstyle and talent, making him one of the most successful and fashionable stars in the NBA.

2. DeAndre Jordan

If you were looking for NBA players with long dreadlocks, who better than DeAndre Jordan? In 2016, the star flaunted his new dreadlocks, a look he still rocks in 2024.

The star is among the top NBA players with dreads in the west. However, DeAndre's dreads are a little shorter this season than he had a few years back.

3. Derrick Rose

Derrick Martell Rose is an American professional basketball player for the New York Knicks. He is among the top NBA players with dreadlocks. Derrick used to spot short hair (buzz cut) before moving to afro fade and now dreadlocks.

4. Markelle Fultz

Markelle N'Gai Fultz is a point guard for the Orlando Magic. He started playing college basketball for the Washington Huskies before being selected by the Philadelphia 76ers as the first overall pick in the 2017 NBA draft.

Markelle's hairstyle has mostly stayed the same since his debut in 2017. While in college, he had an afro fade, then baby locks (during his draft in 2017) and finally, long dreadlocks.

5. Anthony Davis

Anthony Marshon Davis Jr is among the top NBA players with braids. He is a power forward and centre for the Los Angeles Lakers of the NBA. Davis is an eight-time NBA All-Star and has appeared in four All-NBA First Teams and four NBA All-Defensive Teams. But apart from his career, Davis is known for his looks. He has spotted various hairstyles in his career, including afro fade, cornrows, twits and off-scalp braids.

7. Jimmy Butler

If you were looking for NBA players with long hairdos, who better than Jimmy Butler III? He plays as a small forward for the Miami Heat. Jimmy started as the last pick of the first round, being selected by the Chicago Bulls.

In 2023, Jimmy Butler ranks among the NBA's top defenders and one of the most popular players in the league.

8. Elfrid Payton

Elfrid Payton Jr is among the NBA players with short dreads. He is a point guard for the Fort Wayne Mad Ants of the NBA G League. Payton has played for the Orlando Magic, Phoenix Suns, New Orleans Pelicans, New York Knicks and the Phoenix Suns. His hairstyle was freeform dreads, but he cut them in 2018. However, in 2024, the star regained his hair and spotted short dreads.
