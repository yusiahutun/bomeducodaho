Timothee Chalamet is responsible for Jennifer Lawrence's most 'miserable' day ever

May 2024 · 3 minute read

Timothee Chalamet has finally opened up about what happened on Jennifer Lawrence’s ‘most annoying day ever’ on the set of the film, Don’t Look Up.

The Hollywood actress spoke about working with Chalamet and Dicaprio at the end of 2021 on Late Night with Stephen Colbert, revealing that the duo “drove her crazy” that day.

Timothee Chalamet had ‘far too much energy’ annoying Jennifer Lawrence

The film was shot in the times when the pandemic was still rife, and Chalamet had actually spent 14 days in quarantine before he arrived on the movie set, he told GQ.

The star said he “exploded out the room,” when he was finally allowed free from quarantine.

“By the time I got to set I was buzz-ing. That was the day Jennifer said was the most annoying day of her life, working with me and Leo,” the 27-year-old explained.

Speaking on her previous TV appearance, Lawrence said: “Timothée was just excited to be out of the house, I think it was his first scene,” and she was sure right about that one.

Despite having the most ‘annoying’ day of her life, Lawrence holds no bad blood, admitting it was herself that was actually a “misery” that day.

“I just remember being in absolute misery that day. But they’re very nice,” she said of the duo.

The 33-year-old also spoke about how excited she was to work with Chalamet, adding how talented he is.

The actor is keeping his ‘love life’ on the down low

The Dune star has been opening up about certain things in his new GQ interview, but it seems like his love life isn’t one of them, although he give a slight nod to rumored boo, Kylie Jenner.

Chalamet spoke about his wish to live a private life. Of course, interviewer Daniel Riley jokingly said he wouldn’t necessarily hang out with one of the four most-followed people on Instagram, if that’s what he wanted.

“This reminds me of that recent South Park episode with the Worldwide Privacy Tour,” he laughed. “Sometimes, people are going to be hella confused when you say you’re trying to live a private life.”

The actor has been rumored to be dating the youngest KarJenner sister, Kylie Jenner, since the start of this year.

However, they remained just rumors until they sent Twitter into a meltdown in September, going public with their romance at the Beyonce concert. Before this, they were pictured once at a BBQ in very blurry pics, however, this was the first real ‘confirmation’ for fans.

In the videos, the two were seen getting cozy and chatting and laughing in their private balcony. Many celebrities attended the birthday concert for the star that day, including Meghan Markle.

They then took their PDA to the next level at the US Open in September, not shying away from kissing and cuddling in front of the cameras.

However, as of yet, neither has publically spoken about the relationship.
