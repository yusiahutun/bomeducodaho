Formation and Founding

Vissel Kobe was founded in 1966 and is based in Kobe, Japan. The club was established with the aim of promoting football in the region and has become one of the most successful teams in the J1 League.

Home Stadium

Vissel Kobe plays its home matches at the Noevir Stadium Kobe, which has a seating capacity of over 30,000 spectators. The stadium provides a vibrant atmosphere for the fans, creating an unforgettable experience for all who attend.

Ownership

Vissel Kobe is owned by the Rakuten group, a Japanese e-commerce company. Rakuten’s investment in the club has helped attract top international players and establish Vissel Kobe as a formidable force in Japanese football.
