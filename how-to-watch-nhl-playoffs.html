If you follow sports closely, you’ll know that it’s time for postseason hockey. Fans all around the world will be sitting on the edge of their seats during the NHL playoffs. Whether you’re new to the sport or have been following it since forever, you’re likely wondering how to watch the NHL playoffs live for free to see who takes home this year’s Stanley Cup trophy.

The Stanley Cup playoffs is how the National Hockey League (NHL) determines the league champion each year. The elimination tournament is named after the trophy that the winning team receives. The NHL playoffs first took place as an event held on a challenge basis in 1893. It became a prearranged inter-league competition in 1915 and then became known solely as the NHL postseason in 1927. The Las Vegas Knights are the reigning champions and qualified for the 2024 Stanley Cup playoffs. However, the Montreal Canadiens have the most championship titles than any other NHL team (24).

16 teams will compete in the first round of the 2024 NHL playoffs, starting in late April. Following the first round is the second round and then the conference championships (one team wins the Eastern Conference and one team wins the Western Conference). The 2023-2024 NHL season culminates in the conference champions going head to head in the Stanley Cup Finals in early June.

Calling all hockey fans, it’s time for the NHL postseason, and you’re not going to want to miss a single second of the action. So, continue reading to discover how to watch the NHL playoffs to stay up-to-date on which teams are inching closer and closer to the coveted Stanley Cup trophy.
