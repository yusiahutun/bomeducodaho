In the video for California Gurls, Katy sported her iconic whipped cream boobs – a red and white spiral attached to each breast to squirt the delicious dairy product.

The star explained to MTV News about the bizarre video: “These whipped-cream [boobs] were in the music video because that was my only ammo.

“I had to slay those Gummi Bears that were very evil. I had to show them who was boss in Candyfornia. The California Gurls, of course.”

But it wasn’t all rainbows and sunshine, as Katy explained some of her bras were pretty heavy to heft around, adding: “It was really intense with all that whipped cream coming from my back through my assets.

“I will tell you that those cupcake-bra boobies, they were a little bit heavy and they were interesting.”

Naked bathtime

For her music video This Is How We Do, Katy stripped totally naked save for a huge pearl necklace around her throat and a cropped black bob wig.

She then clambered into a bathtub filled to the brim with tiny pearls and sprinkled them over her bare torso while gazing, lips parted, at the camera.

The star explained: “We are having so much fun because there are so many incredible looks and designs and costumes and fashion pieces.

“It’s a great combination of my love for Tumblr and modern art museums.”

It was later claimed that Taylor Swift – amid her high-profile feud with Katy – copied her idea and made it a little more expensive for her Look What You Made Me Do video, bathing instead in a bath of diamonds.

Jungle bikini

Though Katy stripped totally naked to bathe with elephants in her video for Roar, it was her jungle-inspired bikini that had people talking.

As she sings: “I got the eye of the tiger!”, Katy emerges from a cave in a tiny leopard-print bra and a thigh-high skirt constructed entirely from leaves.

The effect left her bare tummy on display as she clutched a spear close to her chest and embraced the outdoorsy look, swinging from vines and dancing among the trees.

NSFW cooking video

In 2017, Katy dropped the music video for Bon Appétit featuring Migos – and she knocked the heat right up during the steamy clip.

The video sees Katy form the centrepiece of a three-course meal, covered in flour by a range of sexy chefs before she’s boiled, basted and surrounded by veggies.

Katy’s long blonde locks are finally chopped off and she’s garnished on a dining table before being set upon by a whole horde of starving foodies.

Katy announced the video on social media with the caption: “Hot and ready to serve, but make no mistake I’m not your piece of [meat].”

Naked babe

In 2020 it was time for Katy to strip completely naked for her Daisies video – while heavily pregnant.

The star can be seen slowly stripping off under a waterfall, cradling her blossoming baby bump and covering her boobs with nothing but a bare arm cinched over her chest.

Katy turned to the side to avoid flashing the camera as she got dripping wet for the stunning song.

Of course, the song was in tribute to her future daughter Daisy, who she shares with Orlando Bloom.

Orlando also shares his son Flynn with his ex-wife Miranda Kerr.
