UsWeekly was the first to report that Seann wed. News broke over the weekend.

On September 11 he told the same outlet that he had been 'dating somebody' but he declined to name the woman. 

His ex: Before his recent nuptials, Scott was engaged to model Lindsay Frimodt, a decision he confirmed in 2012. Seen in 2013

Scott said that his secret sweetheart was 'a great girl.' 

He also mentioned that even though he's a 'pretty private' person, he's 'super happy' in the relationship. 

Before his recent nuptials, Scott was engaged to model Lindsay Frimodt, a decision he confirmed in 2012.

'I don’t how I got a girl like my fiancée,' he said to E! News in jest after he proposed to her. 

'She hadn’t seen American Pie. Thank God. Otherwise, I probably wouldn’t be engaged.'

At the time a source told Us that Scott and Frimodt 'were talking about getting married as early as October, but it didn’t happen then.' 

News broke that they had split in January 2013.

His American Pie co-star Tara Reid, 43, told Us Weekly in February about the potential for a fifth American Pie film. 

Reid also divulged that Scott and their American Pie costar Jason Biggs, 41, have had some disagreements along the way.

Keeping mum: The American Pie actor did not disclose who he tied the knot with, but told the outlet on Sept. 11 that he was 'dating somebody' without disclosing her identity. Here he is in a still from 1999's American Pie with Eden Riegel

'Seann and Jason have to get along,' Reid told the outlet. 'There’s a lot of talk about [the fifth film], but there’s always talk about everything, so you never really know until you know. I hope so!'

Despite Reid's comments, a source told Us Weekly at the time that there is 'absolutely no bad blood between the two.' 

Reid, Biggs, and Scott co-starred in 1999’s American Pie, 2001’s American Pie 2, 2003’s American Wedding and 2012’s American Reunion.
