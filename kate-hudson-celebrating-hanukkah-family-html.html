Kate Hudson celebrated her first night of Hanukkah with her family on Thursday.

The Hollywood star, 44, shared a sweet clip as she cuddled up to her kids Bingham, 12, and Rani Rose, five, during the special celebration.

The Glass Onion favourite continued her long-time family tradition as she captioned: 'Connecting to love, light, miracles and peace tonight on the first day of Hanukkah. Happy Hanukkah everyone.'

Kate was every inch of the doting mum as she couldn't stop smiling while recording herself and the children lighting the first candle on her menorah.

In a sweet moment the actress helped little Rani to place the candles in the right candlesticks, and after holding Bingham to her in a heartfelt hug.
