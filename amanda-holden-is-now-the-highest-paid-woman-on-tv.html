Amanda Holden is now officially the highest earning woman on TV

April 2024 · 2 minute read

Now THAT'S a pretty penny…

    Amanda Holden is the newly crowned queen of TV! The fabulous judge has reportedly overtaken Holly Willoughby as the highest-paid female star on-air, and her annual earnings will make your eyes water. Of course, the blockbuster ITV talent show wouldn't be the same without her, but it's not just a spot on one of the biggest shows on telly that keeps Amanda busy.

    The doting mum-of-two is also a co-host on radio, has starred in adverts for retail giant and has her very own interiors range – not to mention the chic clothing collection she recently launched with luxury British clothing line . How on earth she juggles all that, we'll never know. With all of her ventures combined, it's reported that Amanda draws in a whopping £5 million a year! How's that for hustling?
