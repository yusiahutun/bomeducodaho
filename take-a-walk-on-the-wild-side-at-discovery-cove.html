Take a Walk on the Wild Side at Discovery Cove

July 2024 · 2 minute read

Take summer vacation to the next level with Discovery Cove’s Ultimate VIP Experience package, offering unprecedented service and access to elite benefits.

Take advantage of everything this Central Florida oasis has to offer. The secluded, all-inclusive day park welcomes visitors into a lush island setting and promises immersive animal experiences. Guests can swim with dolphins, snorkel among tropical fish and rays, hand-feed exotic birds, and more, all while enjoying endless complimentary food and drinks.

Discovery Cove combines all-inclusive amenities with “bucket list” encounters and limits the number of daily visitors to ensure an intimate VIP experience. The new Ultimate VIP Experience package ups the luxurious ante, providing personalized VIP Concierge Services and exclusive benefits for up to eight guests.

Perks include full-day access to a VIP Cabana, complete with towel service, snack basket, a stocked mini fridge, dedicated cabana host, as well as a digital photo package and personalized buoy delivery (buoy included with signature dolphin swim purchase only). Need help planning your visit? A VIP Concierge can help tailor the perfect schedule based on your group’s preferences, then serve as a dedicated guide to maximize the fun.

Take a walk on the wild side with a zoological expert guiding you through Explorer’s Aviary, Flamingo Encounter, The Grand Reef, and Snorkeling with Sharks.

Discovery Cove is offering up to 40 percent savings on reservations. Reservations can be made for any date through March 9, 2024, but must be booked by July 23. The package requires a minimum two-day advanced reservation. For more information, or to book the Ultimate VIP Experience package, click here or call (407) 370-1427.
