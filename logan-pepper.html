Career and Professional Life

Professionally, Logan Pepper is an actor. He has been featured in American Housewife, NCIS: Naval Criminal Investigative Service, and American Dad!

His character is Oliver’s uber-wealthy best friend whose parents are almost always out of town. Oliver is played by Daniel DiMaggio. Moreover, his character also adores Katie as a mother figure, played by Katy Mixon.
