The Masked Singer's Jaime Winstone sizzles as she rocks sheer cut-out BRITs dress

March 2024 Â· 2 minute read

The Masked Singer's Jaime Winstone flashed her curves as she hit the BRITs red carpet in a daring, sparkly black dress.

The Kidulthood actress, 36, was one of the many celebs who stepped out at the event at the O2 Arena in London on Tuesday night.

And she made sure all eyes were on her as she walked the carpet in a short, glittering black number that clung to her curves.

The brief dress skimmed Jaime's thighs and left her long legs on show.

Its top featured a sheer cut out that swirled from one shoulder, across her front and down to the opposite hip, showing off Jaime's toned figure as she posed for the cameras on the red carpet.

Jaime added a splash of colour with a green, feathered stole that wound around her neck and down her arms and brought out the shimmering green make-up that sparkled around her eyes.

Her wavy dark hair was loose and tousled around her face, and her long nails were painted a dramatic dark berry shade.

Seriously chunky black shoes that laced part of the way up Jaime's legs completed the show-stopping look.

The After Hours star later shared a series of pictures and videos on her Instagram Story showing her rubbing shoulders with some big names at the glitzy awards ceremony.

One snap showed her beaming as she stood next to Friends star Courteney Cox as they posed backstage together.

"Obsessed," Jaime captioned the sweet picture.

"Sorry for screaming in your face," she added.

A video showed her dancing with Line Of Duty actress Vicky McClure and Doctor Who star Jodie Whittaker, while another picture showed Jaime hugging Little Simz.

The rapper was one of the winners at the show, scooping the coveted Best New Artist award.

"Queen!!! Congratulations Little Simz," Jaime wrote.
