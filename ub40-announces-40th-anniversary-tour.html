UB40 Announces 40th Anniversary Tour

May 2024 · 2 minute read

LOS ANGELES (CelebrityAccess) UB40 Featuring Ali Campbell and Astro have announced their 40th Anniversary Tour: A Real Labour of Love.

Special guest Shaggy will join on all dates across North America this summer and fall. Steel Pulse and The Green will open on select dates. Please see itinerary below.

Produced by Live Nation, the 15-city outing will kick off Aug. 28 in Toronto and make stops in Boston, Brooklyn, Chicago and more before wrapping Sept. 28 in Las Vegas. The tour will see the artists playing outdoors in amphitheaters across the U.S. and Canada all through August and September.

The founding members of UB40 have sold more than 70 million records.
