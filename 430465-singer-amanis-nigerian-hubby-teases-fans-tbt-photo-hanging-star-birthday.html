Singer Amani's Nigerian Hubby Teases Fans with TBT Photo of Himself Hanging Out with Star on Her Bir

February 2024 · 3 minute read
  • Chinasa shared an old photo of himself spending time with his wife Amani followed by sweet words dedicated to the musician
  • The couple's fans were impressed by the former Songa boss' move and took to the comments section of his post to gush over the two
  • Chinasa and Amani welcomed their first child together in 2020 after a number of years in their union

Celebrated Kenyan songstress Cecilia Wairimu's rich Nigerian hubby Chinasa Udeala has taken to social media to celebrate the star in the best way he knows why.

Cherished memories

The former Safaricom's Songa boss took to his Facebook account on Thursday, October 21, and shared an old photo of himself chilling out with his bae Amani during her birthday a couple of years ago.

Through his caption, Chinasa made it clear that he will always cherish all the memories he created with the Badboy hitmaker.

"My heart is constantly smiling because memories are forever," Chinasa captioned the photo.

Chinasa's post attracted the attention of his fans and those of Amani who camped on the comments section to laud the two.

Quite a number of their followers admitted that they are one of the most private couples who inspire them and would really love to be them when ''they grow up''.

The impressive TBT photo of the couple came about a year after TUKO.co.ke reported the two had been blessed with their first child after years of dating.

Chinasa and Amani welcome their first child

The good news was first made public by Udeala through his official Instagram account.

The rich Nigerian man shared a photo of himself holding his baby, followed by the caption;

''Thank you Jesus for all your goodness. Another Udeala makes an appearance. Happiest man alive.''

His announcement impressed many people specifically their fans who trickled in at the comments section of his post to congratulate the couple on their first baby together.

Chinasa, however, pulled down the post moments later but the good news had already spread like bush fire.

Private celebrity couple

TUKO.co.ke reached out to Chinasa to congratulate him and he confirmed the arrival of the bundle of joy.

The Songa Music boss did not share many details such as the baby's gender, the day he or she was delivered and Amani's current situation.

It is important to note the couple is very private and rarely publicise their issues on social media.

The two have been together for over five years now and have been trying to get a baby in vain, until lately.
