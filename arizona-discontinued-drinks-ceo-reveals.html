Inside AriZona CEO's wall of rare and discontinued drinks - including beverage that has customers de

June 2024 · 3 minute read

A BELOVED drink brand has revealed a secret wall inside the CEO's office that contains rare and discontinued beverages.

AriZona Iced Tea showed off all the throwback items on TikTok, and fans all demanded that one flavor returns to shelves.

In the video, the company (@drinkarizona) flashed a huge closet stacked high with colorful cans of bygone teas and sodas.

There are even some products that never made it to stores, including a root beer float-flavored beverage, the account reveals in the caption.

"What flavor do you want to try," the brand asked its audience.

While the answers in the comments varied, one item stood out above the others: the Cherry Lime Rickey.

Multiple users begged to bring it back with some saying they won't rest until the product is back in stores.

"Will fight for that Cherry Lime Rickey," one user commented.

But some commenters chimed in that fans actually can still get the drinks, but only through online orders from the website.

For $26.99, AriZona lovers can get a pack of 12 carbonated treats.

The tall cans are a fun take on the classic Cherry Lime Rickey beverage which was normally made with soda water, lime juice, and maraschino cherry syrup.

"This original Brooklyn beverage is slightly carbonated with a well-balanced mix of sour and tart from lime plus the sweet taste of cherry," the description of the product reads.

Fans also discussed other flavors on the TikTok that amassed over 10,000 likes.

"Bring back Shaq Soda," one person wrote, referencing a by-gone collaboration with the NBA star.

Shaq Soda was a series of cream sodas that were produced by AriZona Beverage Co.

Each soda was either infused with the flavor of strawberry, orange, vanilla, or blueberry.

The calls for change came just after AriZona announced some new summer favorites that can be purchased for under $4.

The four cooler staples have an alcoholic twist just in time for a beach trip this summer.

The brand, known for its canned iced tea beverages, has added three new alcoholic drinks to its lineup.

The drinks, which could be compared to Twisted Tea, are made with real fruit juice, real brewed tea, and star “the same iconic taste AriZona is known for with 5 percent alcohol,” according to a press release.

The flavors are as follows:

  • Iced tea with peach
  • Green tea with ginseng flavor and honey
  • Iced tea with lemon

While regular cans of Arizona are popular for their 99 cents price tag, the alcoholic varieties will be $3.49 each for a 22 fl oz can.

That’s more expensive than Twisted Tea, which is $2.99 for a 24 fl oz can.

The drink is currently rolling out at stores nationwide after a successful test run in Florida.

Fans on social media have already started sharing their thoughts on the new drinks, with one writing on Twitter: “Bro this AriZona hard green tea is so good, mmmmm.”

“Hard AriZona tea is amazing and the best thing ever,” raved another.
