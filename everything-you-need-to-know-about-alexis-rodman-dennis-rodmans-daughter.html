Alexis Rodman: Everything You Need To Know About Dennis Rodman's Daughter

June 2024 · 4 minute read

Alexis Rodman is Dennis Rodman’s oldest child. She made him a father. However, her childhood was difficult, as Dennis was mostly absent, affecting their bond. 

Now an adult, Alexis tries to keep her private life under wraps. But people are still curious about the life she lives. Keep reading to discover the details of Alexis Rodman’s life. 

Who Is Alexis Rodman?

Alexis Caitlin Rodman was born on September 28, 1988, to Annie Bakes and Dennis Keith Rodman in Los Angeles, California, USA. The pair are now divorced. However, they are cordial with one another, and Alexis has a good relationship with them. 

Even though Alexis was the first child Dennis welcomed, he was not the best father. He not only made headlines for weird reasons but was also mostly absent from her life. 

Dennis’ absence affected Alexis’ childhood. She would get into fights at the slightest provocation to get his attention, which made her develop anger management issues. Eventually, she even started blaming her mother for her father’s disappearance. Thankfully that storm blew over, and Alexis is now in a better place emotionally with her parents. 

Does Alexis Rodman Have Siblings?

Alexis Rodman has two confirmed step-siblings. Her oldest sibling is Dennis “D.J.” Jr., and her youngest is Trinity. They are both from Dennis’ marriage to his third wife, Michelle Moyer.

Unlike her, Alexis Rodman’s siblings are active on social media. There is not a lot of information on the relationship between the siblings. However, some reports claim the relationship between them is strained. Whether this is true or just a farce can only be confirmed by either of the three, and they don’t talk much about one another. 

Who Is Alexis Rodman’s Mother?

Alexis Rodman’s mother is Annie Baker. She used to be a model. However, her career ended when she got in a car accident. 

She walked away from the ordeal alive, but she quit modeling after. Instead, she became an author with several books now credited to her. 

Baker’s first book was released in 1997 and was titled “Worse Than He Says He Is: My Walk on the Wild Side With Dennis Rodman.” The book revealed intimate details about her rocky relationship with Alexis. The second book she wrote was titled “White Girls Don’t Bounce.”

Does Alexis Rodman Have Instagram?

What Is Alexis Rodman’s Net Worth?

Alexis Rodman’s net worth is unknown because she is so good at keeping her personal life private. Alexis is known to be living well. However, what she does to make money is unconfirmed. 

Who Is Alexis Rodman’s Husband?

Alexis Rodman is married to Robert Bunfill. The couple tied the knot on August 30, 2014, and the ceremony was held in California. They were blessed with a son named Vincent in 2017, making Dennis Rodman a granddad.

The NBA legend took to Twitter to announce the news in June of that year. He wrote: “Wow, congrats to my daughter Alexis and her husband for the birth of their 1st baby: Vincent! I can’t believe I’m a Grandfather!”

Alexis Rodman’s Height

How Did Alexis Rodman’s Parents Meet?

Alexis Rodman’s parents met at a club in Los Angeles in 1987. Her beauty enamored the retired NBA star, so he walked up to her, and they talked. Baker quickly warmed up to him, and they started going out. The pair lived together in the early days of their relationship, and they welcomed Alexis in 1988, 

She was just a few months old when Baker and Dennis first separated. It was for a short while, and when they reconciled, they decided to tie the knot. They married on September 28, 1991, in South Lake Tahoe, Nevada.
