Taylor Swift Wore a Buttery Yellow Bikini on Vacation

February 2024 · 3 minute read

I don’t have a beach vacation planned, but Taylor Swift just made me reconsider that. Seeing pictures of her island getaway with Travis Kelce immediately had me searching for flights to sunnier places with warm water — and also for that butter-yellow bikini.

Her two-piece swimsuit was, to put it simply, perfection, from the color to the coverage to the details. The soft yellow offered just the right amount of vibrancy, her bottoms were cheeky without being a thong, and the top’s bow detail made the entire look unbelievably cute. So of course I set out to find lookalike styles, and discovered six similar sets that start at just $25.

Zaful Tie-Front Bikini Set

Zaful’s $25 yellow bikini set is nearly identical to Swift’s style, featuring the same underwire design and tie at the front, while the bottoms also include drawstrings that allow you to adjust the level of cheekiness. While the suit might be cheap, shoppers say that they’re impressed with the quality. One person said that they’ve worn the suit over 10 times, a testament to its “good quality,” while a shopper with a 32DDD chest said that it works “pretty well for larger busts,” noting that while it might not offer serious levels of support, they’re also “not spilling out of it.”

Beach Riot Ribbed Bikini Top and Bottoms

If you prefer a touch more coverage when it comes to swimwear, this ribbed set from Beach Riot is a great option. Shoppers say the high-waisted bottoms offer more modesty without looking “frumpy.” “These are sexy,” wrote one fan who raved that they “fit really well and hug [their] body.” Customers share that same sentiment when it comes to the tie-front top, with one person writing that it’s “super sexy but still feels safe [because of] the support from the wide back.” And per someone with a 34DD chest, it “fits beautifully,” adding that it “covers enough for there to not be any side boob or bulging.”

Galamaar Retro Top and High-Waisted Bottoms

This set from Galamaar feels very Taylor Swift, from that buttery yellow shade to the retro cut. The top features slight padding and a hook closure while the bottoms are high-waisted and slightly cheeky; it almost looks like vintage lingerie. And according to shoppers, the fit is “cute and flattering” while the “really soft” yellow enhances a tan — which, as a pale girl, is what I like to hear.

Swift’s yellow bikini made me want to book a spring break trip just so I have an excuse to wear it. Shop more lookalikes, below.
