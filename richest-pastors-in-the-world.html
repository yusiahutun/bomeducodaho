Although wealthy, Jakes has not forgotten his call to help people. Jakes is known for his charitable work, especially in his work empowering black people. One example of Jakes’ work is his initiative with his daughter Sarah Jakes Robert, which seeks to empower Black businesswomen.

Rick is an American pastor and author. He became interested in pastoring at 19. Later, he cofounded his church with his wife called Saddleback Church in Lake Forest, California. Besides being a pastor, he has also written famous books titled “The Purpose Driven Church” and “The Purpose Driven Life.” These books highlight what his church stands for and have been standouts in self-help and faith circles for many years.
