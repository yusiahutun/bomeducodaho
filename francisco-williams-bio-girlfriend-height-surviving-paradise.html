Meet Francisco Williams! His Girlfriend, Height

On October 20, 2023, Netflix’s Surviving Paradise made its debut. Francisco Williams is a cast member. He thought he and the other Surviving Paradise cast members were at an extravagant home. However, when they discovered the truth, they encountered terror and had their jewels and phones taken away.

Find out more about his family, dating life, age, and more from this article below.

Francisco Williams On Netflix’s Surviving Paradise

Francisco Williams is one of the cast members on Netflix’s Surviving Paradise.

Unaware partygoers believe they are in for the summer of a lifetime, but Surviving Paradise has other ideas. The players are almost instantly exiled to the wilderness below it, where they will have to make do on nearly nothing, as opposed to living it up in a luxurious villa on a clifftop overlooking the ocean. The turn? The participants can alter their position in the game and return to the resort to compete for a life-changing cash reward if they can out-plan and out-strategize their rivals.

But nobody is ever safe in this kind of competitive gaming because one minute you’re living it up and the next you’re down to the fundamentals.

Twelve candidates think they’re going to spend the summer in luxury at a gorgeous estate. However, they will have to start from scratch and eke out an existence in the woods without any luxuries before they can pop any bottles. The players living in the wilderness, also known as the “outsiders,” will have numerous opportunities to come back to the villa, where they will eventually become “insiders.”

Outsiders can be voted back into the resort, where they will take an insider’s position and have the opportunity to fight for the cash prize if they succeed in challenges and forge powerful enough coalitions.

The contestants will fight their way into the villa for a chance to win the $100,000 grand prize.

Shea Foster, Aaron Blake, Copan Combs, Francisco Williams, Hayley Smith-Rose, Sarah Kate Reynolds, Justin Assada, Lellies Santiago, Taylor Olympios, Alex Dourassof, Linda Okoli, and Tabitha Kimberly Eva are the cast members competing for the cash prize.

Francisco Williams Family

Francisco Williams lost his father to cancer when he was only 1 year old. He grew up in a two-bedroom apartment with my six siblings.

Francisco and his siblings were raised by his single mother. He gushed, “I watched this woman do things and bust her ass like no other to make sure that we had a roof over our head, even tho we ain’t have. To the point she ended up getting sick. Once she got sick my entire life changed. From my behavior towards her to the way I acted in school. Even to me running the streets to make money just to help provide for my family.”

Franciso is of Trinidad & Tobago and Jamaican by heritage. In August 2019, Francisco shared that he is the first in his family to graduate from college. He gushed, “👨🏾‍🎓My Degree 📜 finally came in the mail 😭😭 Being the first one in my family to ever Graduate 🎓 college is still one of my biggest accomplishments, with honors at that 🙌🏾🤧❤️ this is something I’ll be able to show my children one day 🙌🏾 #smilesome #didthat.”

What Job Does Francisco Williams Do For A Living?

Francisco Williams is an entrepreneur and entertainer by profession. However, he has come a long way while overcoming a life of struggle.

In one IG post, Francisco wrote, “I have been homeless, I’ve been defeated and I went through hell. All I ever cared about was making sure my loved ones were good. With all that being said. I just want to let anyone out there know that.”

“With all that being said. I just want to let anyone out there know that. There’s a light at the end of the tunnel. No matter how dark it may seem. & anything you want to do in this life you can achieve it. I’m living proof. Thank you to those that’s been rocking with me since day 1, it has been a crazy journey. This is only the beginning and I have so much more to accomplish & show the world 🌎 💫🖤 so thanks for the support, I hope you guys are enjoying the journey because it’s about to be a movie 🎥 N I’m glad y’all are able to witness it all with me 💫 Love is love it’s time, the Revolution shall be televised 📺🖤 “Boy Meets World 🌎.” he concluded.

Francisco graduated from Benjamin Cardozo High School where he was a basketball star. For college, he went to Bluefield University. He also played basketball at Niagara County Community College.
