41 years old Venezuelan actor Edgar Ramirez is a bonafide bachelor. He made the news after his kiss with Ana de Armas went viral in social media. He has a special relationship with his telenovela co-star Marisa Roma.

Quick Facts: Edgar Ramirez

  • Born as Édgar Filiberto Ramírez Arellano to father Filiberto Ramirez and mother Soday Arellano on March 25, 1977.
  • Born in San Cristóbal, Táchira, Venezuela.
  • His father was a military officer and mother was an attorney.
  • He has a sister named Nataly.
  • He was raised as Roman Catholic.
  • He is fluent in five languages: Spanish, German, English, French, and Italian. 
  • His childhood was spent in various countries.
  • He graduated in the degree of mass communication from the Andrés Bello Catholic University in 1999.
  • He has also received the degree in politics and worked as political reporter and commentator for some time.
  • He minored in Audio-visual communications.
  • He was in-charge of Viart Film Festival.
  • He was appointed UNICEF goodwill ambassador.
  • He made his motion picture debut in 2005 with Domino.
  • His net worth is estimated to be above $5 million. 
  • His early salary is estimated to be around $606,000.
