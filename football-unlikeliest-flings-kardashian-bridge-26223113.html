Cristiano Ronaldo and Gemma Atkinson

Look who it is! Making his second entry into our list of unlikely flings, it's Ronaldo, and this time it concerns his brief dalliance with ex-Hollyoaks star Gemma Atkinson.

The pair struck up an unlikely bond just after Atkinson had left the iconic Channel 4 show, where she played the role of Lisa Hunter.

But despite Ronaldo's status as a global superstar, his date with the 37-year-old actress was surprisingly low key as they had a cup of tea and watched Only Fools and Horses together.

"It was really, really glamorous," the actress said sarcastically.

Speaking about her first date with the United star with Manchester-based radio station Key 103, Atkinson said: "We went to my house and drank cups of tea and watched Only Fools and Horses. Genuinely."

That sounds lovely jubbly to us, but their liaison didn't last as Atkinson revealed how the star was hounded by paparazzi during their time together.

"With Cristiano it was mad, because everywhere he went he was followed by photographers. When we did split up I got offered so much money to speak about him and I didn't want that," she said.
