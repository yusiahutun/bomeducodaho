Queen + Adam Lambert To Debut Brand New Rhapsody Touring Show

April 2024 · 2 minute read

LOS ANGELES (CelebrityAccess) – With Bohemian Rhapsody – the Freddie Mercury/Queen movie – set to become the biggest grossing music biopic of all time, it’s clear that the public’s love for Queen remains as strong as ever.

With this in mind, Queen + Adam Lambert have today (Dec. 3) announced that they will return to North America next summer to debut an all-new concert experience. Produced by Live Nation, the 23-date arena and stadium Rhapsody tour is set to run from mid-July through late August 2019.

Going under the Rhapsody header, the six-week tour will debut a brand new larger-than-life production. Stufish Entertainment Architects’ set designer Ric Lipson said: “Queen + Adam Lambert’s Rhapsody tour will, once again for them, expand the parameters of what a live music experience can be. This new show promises to be their most spectacular yet, an innovative and interactive celebration of the continued power of Queen’s music.”

The tour follows an acclaimed run of 10 sold-out shows at the MGM Park Theater, Las Vegas in September 2018 and will kick off July 10th at Vancouver’s Rogers Arena. Stop-offs include The Forum in Los Angeles (the last venue the original line-up played in North America on September 15, 1982) and New York City’s Madison Square Garden among others.

Tickets for the Rhapsody tour go on sale to the general public starting December 7th at LiveNation.com at 10AM local time.
