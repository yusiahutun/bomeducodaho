List of interior design companies in Kenya

February 2024 · 7 minute read

Interior designing is a form of art which is used to style and improve the design of your home. Interior designers are creative people and they are organized and disciplined businessperson with an aesthetic perception; hired to change the way the inside of a house looks like. In this article, we will talk about interior design companies in Kenya. If you live in Kenya and you are looking for the best company to style your home’s interior, check out our list of companies and decide for yourself.

Best interior design companies in Kenya

In Kenya, interior designers work in both residential and commercial settings. Many Kenyan designers practice in both commercial and residential settings, even though they finally do either of the two. In Kenya, interior designers need to be knowledgeable regarding the business needs of clients. It is because of this reason the designers there focus on specializations. For instance, some designers decide to work to design hospitals or other health care sectors, while some may prefer restaurant designing.

There are a lot of different areas of specialization in interior designing. Some may be highly specialized while others may be simpler.

Kenslay interiors

Kenslay Interiors is held by Ted Macharia and Eric Onsarigo. Their designer style mainly concentrates on the elegant urban styling of interiors. They finish their creative work with a mix of varied textures and careful marshalling of scales, thus creating something unique and unpredictable.

Although mostly minimalistic, they do know where they need to throw in a pinch of bright colour and make their simple design look eye catchy. If you did not know, the Governor’s office in Nyamira County is one of their many projects. Grand Thornton, Mirage building, and The One Beauty Spa are some of their other designer projects.

Interior places

Based in Nairobi, Interior Places is but a 27-people designing company. They have worked on many projects such as Revitalize Health and Wellness Center, the Oval at Westlands, Airtel Africa. They are also well known for having worked on interiors of celebrity houses like Dj Mista Dru, and Oga Obinna.

They have different projects that include lighting, interior finishing products, paintwork, and gypsum ceiling.

African Eye Crafts

An interior decoration company in Kenya, African Eye Crafts deal in creation and designing of unique home decoration. They also provide branding and decoration ideas to house owners and corporate alike. Their products mainly include wall murals mirrors and vinyl.

Zidaka Interiors

Incorporated in the year 2008, Zidaka Homes Ltd, under Zidaka Interiors is an interior design firm working for hospitality spaces, commercial spaces, and private homes. Ever since its establishment, the company has grown immensely. They look forward to co-operating with clients to create something singular and totally personalized according to the clients’ wants.

Their vision is to become an international Interior Design House and be a trendsetter in the market for interior designing and be the choice both locally and regionally.

Pulsaris Designs

Pulsaris Designs is an interior design firm that can make your project happen right from the concept until providing a full turnkey solution. Situated in Mombasa, Pulsaris Designs provide sophisticated and functional solutions to residential, corporate, and commercial spaces. They can custom build furniture and fittings or purchase them directly from your favourite retailer matching your budget and preferences accordingly.

Unity Makers

Unity Makers are a team of modern interior designers who make modern designer furniture for their clients. They can work on making you furniture for dining room, living room, bedroom, gardens etc. Additionally, they also build and design custom furniture.

Whatever your need be, if you are looking for a taste of modern designing, they can create things that are beautiful and suits your style and space.

Creo Interiors

Creo Interiors has its headquarters in Nairobi and operates all over East Africa. Their expertise is in interiors, custom fabrication, signage, visual displays, and kiosks. Creo Interiors design and deliver anywhere in East Africa.

Smart Focus Estate

Well managed, Smart Focus caters for a variety of services starting from Construction of Apartments, maintenance of rental houses, management of rental houses, purchase and sale of land, gardening, and provision of security guards.
