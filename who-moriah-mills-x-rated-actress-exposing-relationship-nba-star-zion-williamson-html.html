Will this saga lead to Zion being traded? 

Mills' allegations couldn't have come at a worse time for Williamson, with the NBA Draft taking place on Thursday. The Pelicans have drafted in the lottery ever year since selecting Williamson, four years ago. 

New Orleans holds the No. 14 pick in the upcoming draft. However, the franchise is said to be left unsatisfied with the 14th pick and its front office is reportedly trying to aggressively trade up before draft night, eyeing the second or third lottery pick instead.

Williamson was also among multiple trade targets that the Portland Trail Blazers have discussed in pre-draft meetings this week with NBA free agency right around the corner. Portland owns the third overall pick in the draft and various outlets rate the likelihood of Williamson being available with degrees of confidence.
