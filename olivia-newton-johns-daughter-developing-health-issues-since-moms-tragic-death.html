Olivia Newton-John's daughter developing 'health issues' since mom's tragic death

June 2024 · 3 minute read

Olivia Netwton-John’s daughter Chloe Rose Lattanzi has opened up to her Instagram followers about how she’s been coping after her mom’s death, stating that she’s been going through her own ‘health issues’ in her mind and body. The star also said she’d been ‘neglecting herself’ and is on a mission to change that over the coming months.

The Hollywood star tragically passed away in August of last year at the age of 73, after a long battle with cancer. Fans across the globe mourned the loss of the Grease actor.

Olivia Newton-John’s daughter Chloe ‘not been feeling okay’ since mom’s death

Olivia Newton-John’s daughter, Chloe Rose Lattanzi took to her 142k Instagram followers to document how she’d been feeling since her mom’s death just under a year ago.

In the video, Chloe explained how she had “not been feeling okay”. She then went on to say she’s had “extreme memory loss” as well as “difficulty getting out of bed”.

The music star said she had stuck to her commitments, however had been “neglecting herself” in the grieving process.

Chloe then went on to explain that one of her mother’s biggest messages was: “Take care of you. If you don’t take care of you, you cannot give your full capacity of love, wisdom, kindness and power to everyone else.”

Olivia’s daughter is taking part in a Walk for Wellness event honoring Newton-John on Oct. 8, however, said she’s going to “disappear” for a month after then.

“After the walk I’m going to disappear for about three weeks, just to honor my mind, body and spirit because I’m developing a little bit of health issues in my mind and my body,” Lattanzi explained.

“I haven’t been consistent with myself and I haven’t been taking care of myself. So in the spirit of my mother and the spirit of the Wellness Walk, I’m going to take a month to do that so that I can be of complete service to everyone else,” she concluded.

Fans rush to support Chloe

Upon seeing Chloe open up in an emotional video on their timelines, fans and celebrity faces rushed to support the Dancing With The Stars alum in the comments by offering words of support.

“Absolutely – your Mom wants you to take good care of you so please do that & remember she’s always with you,” wrote one supportive fan.

Another penned: “Hooray! Take all the time that you need. You are number one. You are growing in leaps and bounds. Your mama would be so proud. We are so proud, and we love you.”

“This just shows how big your heart is. The bigger the heart, the bigger the break. You lost your Mum! Thats the biggest loss to a daughter, ever! Apart from losing your own child. You’re amazing,” wrote another.
