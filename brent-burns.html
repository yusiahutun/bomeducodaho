Biography

Brent Burns was born in Barrie, Canada on March 9, 1985. Defender and occasional winger who made his NHL debut in 2003 for the Minnesota Wild and later joined the San Jose Sharks in 2011. He helped the Sharks reach the 2016 Stanley Cup Finals and in 2017, he received the Norris Trophy, awarded to the league’s top defender. Winger Marian Gaborik was his teammate on the Wild. He played for the Couchiching Terriers in the Ontario Junior Hockey League.
