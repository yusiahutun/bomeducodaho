“Unlike regular radiofrequency or microneedling treatments, Morpheus8 delivers RF energy through tiny needles that penetrate deeper layers of the skin. This allows for precise targeting of problem areas and stimulates collagen production more effectively, leading to better skin texture, tightness and reduced wrinkles,” she said.

What makes Morpheus8 different from its competitors is in its advanced technology, depth control and customisable settings. “Morpheus8 utilises a fractional radiofrequency technology that delivers controlled energy through its microneedles,” Ramirez said.

How does it work?

This cutting-edge treatment extends beyond the contours of the face and neck to embrace the entire body. The secret lies in its masterful ability to trigger your skin to produce collagen and elastin, which leads to better skin texture, smaller-looking pores, vanished acne scars, faded-out fine lines and wrinkles, and an overall tighter, sculpted skin.

For many, the most remarkable outcome of Morpheus8 is the new-found plumpness and reduction in fine lines that linger long after the appointment.

At your appointment, your doctor will glide the device across your skin, with the delicate under-eye region and jawline receiving the spotlight with a few extra minutes of attention. Post-treatment, your skin may showcase a rosy hue that may last around an hour: however, it should naturally dim over the following days.

Who’s a good candidate for the Morpheus8?

According to Ramirez, the treatment is ideal for those seeking skin tightening, wrinkle reduction and skin tone and texture improvement. “It’s especially beneficial for those with mild to moderate skin laxity, wrinkles or uneven skin texture on the face and body.”

While the powers of Morpheus8 aren’t eternal, they certainly make a lasting impression. Results often last for around a year, influenced by your unique capacity to stimulate collagen and your lifestyle choices.

Ramirez also highlights Morpheus8’s adjustable settings for the radiofrequency part of the procedure “to make sure no further damage is done to individuals concerned with hyperpigmentation.

Candidates who want to rejuvenate their appearance without undergoing surgery can benefit from Morpheus8.” Additionally, and as with all facial treatments, patience is key, as most patients can start seeing real results about four to six weeks after the treatment.
