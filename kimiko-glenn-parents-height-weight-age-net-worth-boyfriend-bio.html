Kimiko Glenn Bio, Age

The American actress was born on June 27, 1989, in Phoenix, Arizona, USA, as Kimiko Elizabeth Glenn. She is an American by nationality but belongs to a multiracial ethnic group. Her sun sign is cancer. The aspiring star has a sister named Amanda Glen, who is a talented singer and actress known for her roles in the classic musical Gypsy.

Kimiko Glen attended Desert Vista High School, a public school in Phoenix, Arizona, and later the boarding school of the Interlochen Arts Academy in Interlochen, Michigan. She then went to the Boston Conservatory, a formerly independent conservatory of performing arts in Boston, Massachusetts, which offers degrees in music, drama, and dance to study musical theater. However, she dropped out after a year to join the cast of Spring Awakening in Los Angeles.
