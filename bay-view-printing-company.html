We bound the book ourselves with a stitching technique we were taught by Ashley Town, our teacher and (owner?) of the place.

I like how they are approaching the business. Using what they have to the fullest!

  • They take on printing jobs
  • They help design as well, if needed
  • They print their own art and sell it.
  • They are a community shop, so you can be a member and use the space/equipment
  • They teach classes

They have a pretty extensive collection of wood and metal type (and symbols and flourishes and stuff).

We explored that, came up with an idea, and laid out type to form our designs.

Then we learned about how to lock up the design with furniture (blocks of wood, lower set than the type) so the design stays stable and can be clamped together for printing.

Then we inked up and printed!
