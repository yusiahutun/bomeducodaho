The Real Housewives of Salt Lake City returned with its third season on 28 September 2022. So, it brought with it some new faces to the show, including Angie Katsanevas.

Meet Shawn Trujillo, RHOSLC Star Angie Katsanevas Husband

Shawn Trujillo and RHOSLC star Angie Katsanevas have been married since July 17th of 1999. Since then, many a lot of beautiful things happened to them on the day 17th.

Shawn, citing that the number 17 is his lucky number, once wrote on social media that he was born on the 17th, their daughter was also born on the 17th and were together for 17 years when they had her.

Shawn and Angie were 25 and 28 years old when they started on their journey together. Two young kids chasing a dream, Shawn said looking back. He said they were willing to do the work, and believed that together as a team, they could make anything happen. To put it simply, they were two young kids, who met in a hair salon and fell in love sometime around 1995.

While on Father’s Day, it was Angie’s turn to gush about her husband being the best dad to their daughter, Elektra.

Talking about the science behind choosing one’s mate Angie said as a wild 21-year-old, she was intuitively choosing the future father of her daughter. Handsome, strong, determined, focused, competitive, integral, with big muscles, big goals, and big dreams, Angie worded that, Shawn wanted to be the best at everything he did and when presented to become a Father he did just that.

Recalling Elektra as a newborn baby, born on 17 May 2022, Shawn shared that she came into this world at 7:59 a.m., weighing in at 5.1. A delicate doll-like baby, he said did not cry and was just batting her long eyelashes, staring back at him and bringing her new Mom to tears. Fast forward to today, and she is a champion Equestrian.

About his daughter, Shawn also gushed that his “little animal lover” has already planned to own a Ranch and train horses one day after qualifying as an equestrian in the Olympics.

Shawn Trujillo Job

RHOSLC star Angie Katsanevas and her husband also are business partners. The two have worked together through their hair salon businesses over the years.

As per LinkedIn, Shawn has continued his endeavors as the co-founder of Lunatic Fringe Salons and owner of Paul Mitchell The School SLC since July 1999. The business, that he co-founds with his life partner, may seem like an overnight success now. But there have been 20-plus years of trying and perseverance involved here. The same year they got married, they had decided to open a little salon in a 700-square-foot cottage across Trolley Square. As of 2022 and it has established itself as a fast-growing nationwide salon network branching into nine outlets in Utah, Idaho, and Ohio.

And so like that, Lunatic Fringe Salons, founded by two young hairdressers, is now a nationally recognized brand.
