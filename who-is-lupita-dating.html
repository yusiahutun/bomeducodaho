Who is Lupita Dating? A Timeline of Their Relationship!

April 2024 · 3 minute read

Lupita Nyong’o stands as one of Hollywood’s most gifted and celebrated actresses. She clinched an Oscar for her remarkable performance in “12 Years a Slave” and graced the screens in blockbuster hits like “Black Panther” and “Star Wars.” But who is the Kenyan-Mexican beauty currently in a relationship with? Let’s delve into her dating history and her current romantic status.

Previous Relationships

Throughout her career, Nyong’o has been rumored to be involved with various well-known figures, although none of these relationships were officially confirmed by either her or her representatives. Some of the speculated romances include:

  • Michael Fassbender: Rumors circulated about a potential romance between Nyong’o and her co-star from “12 Years a Slave.” There were reports of flirtatious behavior on set and at award shows in 2013, but neither party addressed the speculations.
  • Jared Leto: Nyong’o and the Oscar-winning actor were said to share on-and-off-screen chemistry, with cozy sightings at multiple events in 2014. However, Nyong’o later clarified that they were simply good friends, dispelling any romantic ties.
  • K’naan: In 2013 and 2014, Nyong’o was frequently spotted with the Somali-Canadian rapper. Their interactions sparked dating rumors, especially when they were photographed holding hands and hugging. Nevertheless, Nyong’o refrained from confirming their relationship status.
  • Michael B. Jordan: During the promotion of “Black Panther” in 2018, Nyong’o and her co-star delighted fans with their adorable interactions. They even shared a kiss on stage at the MTV Movie & TV Awards, but insisted it was a friendly gesture.
  • Janelle Monáe: Rumors swirled about Nyong’o’s relationship with the singer-songwriter after they were seen dancing and getting close at the Met Gala in 2019. An Instagram Live video also hinted at a flirty dynamic. However, neither of them addressed the nature of their relationship.

Current Relationship

Nyong’o’s current partner is Selema Masekela, a multi-talented figure encompassing roles as a TV host, producer, and musician. He is the son of the late South African jazz legend Hugh Masekela. The couple made their relationship public in December 2022 by posting heartfelt messages to each other on Instagram. Nyong’o affectionately referred to Masekela as her “whole and actual love,” while he praised her as his “favorite human.” They also shared photos of their joint Christmas celebration with their respective families.

Though Nyong’o and Masekela have been dating for less than a year, their happiness and love for each other are evident. They actively support each other’s careers and interests, with Nyong’o attending Masekela’s concerts and Masekela cheering on Nyong’o’s projects.

Conclusion

Lupita Nyong’o is undoubtedly one of the most accomplished and esteemed actresses globally. While her professional success is widely recognized, she has maintained a private and low-key personal life. Her past relationships, though rumored, remain unconfirmed.

Her current boyfriend is Selema Masekela, a multi-talented individual and the son of a jazz legend. They have been together since late 2022 and have publicly displayed their affection on social media. This talented and beautiful couple appears to bring joy to each other’s lives.
