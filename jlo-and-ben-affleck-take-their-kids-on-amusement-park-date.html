J.Lo and Ben Affleck Take Their Kids on an Amusement Park Date

May 2024 · 2 minute read

Bennifer brought the family together for a trip to the amusement park.

Jennifer Lopez and Ben Affleck were spotted with their kids at Universal Studios Hollywood yesterday. The photos obtained by Page Six, which you can see here, shows the couple strolling through Springfield, Universal's Simpsons-themed attraction with Lopez's 13-year-old twins Max and Emme, and Affleck's 9-year-old son with ex-wife Jennifer Garner, Samuel. The couple held hands as they and the kids walked through the park, accompanied by bodyguards.

Lopez was cool and casual in a white, cropped tank top and cream drawstring pants, accessorized with sunglasses and a pink crossbody bag.

An onlooker at the park told Page Six that the couple "definitely looked in love," adding, "They looked like they were just two people walking around — not a single person came up to them."

Lopez and Affleck have been seen together around L.A. multiple times since it was reported last month that Lopez and her kids were relocating to be closer to Affleck. Affleck has also been reportedly getting to know Max and Emme. A source told E! News last month that Affleck spent time with Lopez's kids last month at her sister Lynda Lopez's 50th birthday party in Malibu. "Her kids really like Ben and think he's funny and fun to be around. He's totally won the family over with his charm," they said.

Looks like the Lopez family are loving L.A. and the Afflecks.
