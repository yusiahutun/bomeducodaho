Age, Family & Tattoo

Joanne Kelly was born in Bay d’Espoir, Canada on 22 December 1978. As a child, she was raised alongside a brother in a family of four by her parents in Newfoundland and Labrador.

At the age of seventeen, the Bay d’Espoir native moved to Nova Scotia to attend Acadia University for higher studies.

While Joanne is a full-time actress now, she was also up for sports during her high school days and wanted to pursue a career in hockey. She played basketball, volleyball, badminton, and even participated as a track athlete before shifting to acting.

Her first acting role came from a drama film titled The Bay of Love and Sorrows in 2002. Like her tattoo, which reads 'Simply the things I am shall make me live,' her talents spoke for herself in front of the camera.
