Riddhima Kapoor Sahni(Successful Entrepreneur) Wiki, Family, Biography &amp; More

Riddhima Kapoor Sahni is an Entrepreneur and Indian Fashion Designer. She is the sister of Ranbir Kapoor and daughter of the Indian Actor Rishi Kapor.

Personal Life:

Riddhima Kapoor Sahni was born on 15th of September 1980. Her zodiac sign is Virgo and she lives in Mumbai, Maharashtra, India.

Education:

Riddhima Kapoor completed her Bachelors in Designing and Marketing from The American Intercontinental University in London.

Family & Caste:

Riddhima Kapoor Sahni is a member of the Kapoor family of Bollywood. She belongs to a Punjabi Khatri Family.

Parents & Siblings:

Riddhima is the daughter of Neetu Singh and Rishi Kapoor and the sister of Ranbir Kapoor.

She is the granddaughter of the actors, Shammi Kapoor, Shashi Kapoor, and Raj Kapoor; grandniece of Ritu Nanda, Rajiv Kapoor, Randhir Kapoor and Rima Jain. She is the cousin of the bollywood stars Kareena Kapoor, Aadar Jain, Armaan Jain and Karishma Kapoor.

Husband & Children:

Riddhima Kapoor Sahni is the wife of the Indian Businessman Bharat Sahni. Both of them dated for five years and then got married on 25th of January in the year 2006. They have a daughter names Samara Sahni.

Career:

Riddhima Kapoor Sahni started for modelling career for Notandas which is a jewellery brand with her mother, Neetu Singh. She has also walked the ramps for various fashion designer like Manav Gangwani and Suneet Varma.

When she was working for Notandas, she was attracted towards the designs. Later when her modelling contract was over she decided to start designing her own jewellery. In 2016, she launched her own jewellery brand named ‘R Jewellery’ with one of her best friend, Anuj Kapoor of Punjab Jewellers.

In 2016 she designed earrings for the popular American actress Goldie Hawn.

In 2018, she launched her clothing brand named ‘Sam & Friends’ which is named after her daughter Samara.

Awards & Achievements:

  • Femina Power List Award (North) in 2017.
  • Delhiites Lifestyle Award for Jewellery Designer of the Year in 2018.
  • FICCI FLO Delhi Award in 2018.
