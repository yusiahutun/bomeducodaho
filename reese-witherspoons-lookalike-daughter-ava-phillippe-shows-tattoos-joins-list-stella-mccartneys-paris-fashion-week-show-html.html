Ava Phillippe showed off her tattoo collection as she joined the A-list at the Stella McCartney Paris Fashion Week show on Monday morning. 

Reese Witherspoon's lookalike daughter, 24, put on a glamorous display in a flowy white jumpsuit as she watched a presentation of the British designer's Womenswear Spring/Summer 2024 collection.

The chic number featured a one-shoulder neckline and stylish scarf detailing which wrapped around her neck and came down to just below her feet.

Embroidered with gold detailing, the garment also featured a belt detailing, clinching her gorgeous figure at the waist. 

Ava - whom Reese shares with ex-husband Ryan Phillippe - displayed her multiple arm tattoos, including a delicate feather and floral design, as she worked her magic on the red carpet alongside the great and good of the fashion world.

She paired her look with simple brown heels and toted a black clutch bag in hand.

It comes after Reese wished her 'glorious' lookalike daughter Ava a happy 24th birthday on Instagram last month.

Her post included a recent picture of the movie star and her daughter together, demonstrating their uncanny resemblance.

Alongside the snapshot, Reese included a heart-warming throwback of herself with Ava when she was a little girl.

The actress, 47, shares Ava and son Deacon, 19, with her first husband Ryan Phillippe.

'It's the joy of my life to watch you grow and become the most inspiring, thoughtful, creative, dynamic, funny woman,' Reese wrote in her birthday post.

'I have learned so much from being your mom. How lucky is that ??!! I love you to the stars and back!' the Legally Blonde star added.

Taking the show to her Stories, Reese said: 'This adorable little person... Who always makes me laugh and smile. Who loves to have fun and celebrate.... SHE IS 24 TODAY !!!' 

Along with her two older children, Reese also has a son called Tennessee, 11, whom she welcomed with her second husband Jim Toth.

Jim, an ex-talent agent who represented such names as Scarlett Johansson and Matthew McConaughey, married Reese in 2011 at her ranch in Ojai, California.

They announced their separation in March, just two days before they would have celebrated their 12th wedding anniversary.
