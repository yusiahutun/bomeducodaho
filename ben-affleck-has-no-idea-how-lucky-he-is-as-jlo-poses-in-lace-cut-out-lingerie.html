Ben Affleck has no idea how lucky he is as JLo poses in lace cut-out lingerie

June 2024 · 2 minute read

JLo is stunning on the gram once again, as she poses in her new lingerie collection, with Intimissi, and fans are commenting on how ‘lucky’ her husband, Ben Affleck is.

The celebrity couple broke hearts across the globe when they called it quits in 2004 after postponing their wedding. However, they mended all the broken hearts 18 years later, confirming they were back together in July 2021, and finally tying the knot a year later.

JLo poses in lace cut-out lingerie collection

Taking to her whopping 252 million Instagram followers, the Jenny from the Block singer announced the drop of her new lingerie collection with Italian brand, Intimissimi.

If there was any way to announce a collection, it would be this way. The star stunned in a champagne-colored lacy two-piece, which she pairs with a matching silky robe.

Of course, it wouldn’t be a JLo number if she didn’t add some jazz, as the piece comes with a stunning bejeweled harness.

The star can be seen in a number of different poses, from sitting upright, to seductively lying across the couch, in a one-piece from the same collection.

Taking to the caption, the 54-year-old penned: “Introducing my @IntimissimiOfficial THIS IS ME…NOW collection. From Verona to Hollywood and made with love. @NormanJeanRoy #linkinbio”

Speaking on her decision to partner with the luxury brand, the star told The Evening Standard: “Lingerie is deeply intimate – it’s an embrace that empowers you to feel free and beautiful.”

“It’s delicate, it’s soft yet reveals the confidence in yourself.”

Of course, fans ran to the comments of the post when they saw it pop up on their timeline, congratulating the star on her collection, and complimenting her looks.

“Gobsmackingly gorgeous!” exclaimed one.

“You look absolutely amazing [heart eye emojis],” another wrote.

One fan claimed: “Most beautiful woman to walk this earth.”

“BEN AFFLECK IS SO LUCKY!” shouted another.

The pair recently stepped out for a date night together, at L.A. hotspot Peppermint Club, as they both stunned in their fall fits.

A complete contrast to her latest post, the On The Floor singer wore a huge white teddy bear coat, whilst Ben opted for an all-black ensemble.

Opposites attract!
