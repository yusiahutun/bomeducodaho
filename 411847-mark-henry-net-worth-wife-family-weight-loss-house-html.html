Early life

Mark Jerrold Henry was born in June 1971. He is the son of Barbara Jean and Ernest Henry and has one brother, Pat Henry. Unfortunately, his father passed away when Mark was only 12 years old.

Mark comes from a family of heavyweight men. He had a great uncle who weighed 500 pounds. How much does Mark Henry weigh? The strongman weighs a whopping 184kgs. Henry developed an interest in weight lifting from a young age. When he was 18 years old, Los Angeles Times named him the strongest teenager.

Henry attended Silsbee High School. As a freshman, he would squat 270 kg. He was also a member of the school's football team.

Unfortunately, the Texas native dropped out of high school after scoring less than 700 on the SAT (Scholastic Aptitude Test) exam, and he also strained his wrist ligaments. He then channelled all his efforts towards performing, giving him an early start to his career.
